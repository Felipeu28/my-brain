Presentation Notes: Unlocking Growth: AI for Small Business
Buda 2025 Entrepreneur Camp | June 18, 2025
Andres Urrego (MoilApp) &amp; Joshua Edmond (JUCO AI)
Objective: Empower SMBs with actionable AI knowledge, address their struggles, and showcase tools like Grok, ChatGPT, and MoilApp’s free platforms. Inspire action with “no question unanswered in 2025” and “the sky is the limit.”
Total Time: 75 min (5 + 10 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 15 + 6 + 3 + 10)
1. Introduction (5 min, lead)
Goal: Set tone, connect with SMB struggles, emphasize AI urgency.
Welcome: “Good morning! Josh and I are excited for Buda 2025. I’m Andres, founder of MoilApp, helping offline workers and SMBs. Josh founded JUCO AI for community college students.”
SMB Struggles: “Small budgets, small teams, endless tasks—you’re juggling everything. AI is your superpower to save time, cut costs, scale smarter.”
2025 Urgency: “In 2025, no question should go unanswered. Tools like Grok, ChatGPT give instant answers. Don’t get complacent like we did with Google—the sky’s the limit for AI!”
Preview: “We’ll cover what AI is, practical uses, a live landing page demo, and two free MoilApp tools. You’ll leave ready to act.”
Engage: “Raise your hand if you feel overwhelmed by tasks or competing with bigger businesses.”
Transition: “Let’s start with what AI really is. I’ll lead this part.”
2. What is AI? (10 min, lead, including Prompt Engineering)
Goal: Demystify AI, teach prompt engineering.
AI Defined (2 min): “AI mimics human tasks—learns from data to automate, predict, optimize. Think Google Maps, saving 15-20% on routes. SMBs: work smarter, not harder.”
Two Types (2 min):
Generative AI: “ChatGPT, Grok create blog posts, emails, designs.”
Predictive AI: “Forecasts inventory, customer trends.”
SMB Value (2 min): “AI levels the playing field. No big team needed—answer queries at 2 a.m., target customers better.”
Prompt Engineering (4 min):
Why: “Good prompts = great AI results, like clear employee instructions.”
Structure: Role (“marketing expert”), Task (“write blog post”), Details (“coffee shop, young professionals”), Tone (“friendly”), Constraints (“simple, no jargon”).
Example: “Act as a web designer. Create a landing page outline for a bakery, families, warm tone, headline, subheadline, call-to-action, under 200 words.”
Activity: “Pair up, brainstorm a prompt for your business in 30 sec (e.g., social post, expense analysis).”
Success Story: “Restaurant used Grok prompt to create a week’s Instagram posts in 10 min.”
Tools: Grok (free on x.com), ChatGPT—accessible for all.
Engage: “Who’s tried AI like ChatGPT? What worked or didn’t?”
Transition: “Now, Josh will explain why AI is a game-changer for SMBs.”
3. Why AI for Your Small Business? (8 min, Joshua leads, support as needed)
Goal: Highlight AI’s value for SMB struggles.
Your Role: Support Josh, add MoilApp examples if prompted.
Key Points (listen for):
Competitive edge: 3x faster decisions (PwC, 2023).
Cost savings: 15-30% via automation (McKinsey).
Revenue: 10-20% sales boost with personalization.
Efficiency: Automate 40% of tasks (invoicing, scheduling).
Add if Needed: “MoilApp uses AI to streamline hiring, saving SMBs time. JUCO AI personalizes student support—same principle for you.”
Engage: Josh asks, “Which benefit—time, cost, sales—matters most to you?” (Chime in if audience mentions hiring: “MoilApp’s hiring tool helps!”)
Transition: “Next, Josh will cover AI for marketing, but first, any quick questions?”
4. Practical AI Applications: Customers &amp; Marketing (8 min, Joshua leads, support as needed)
Goal: Show marketing tools, emphasize goals to avoid “noise.”
Your Role: Support Josh, add MoilApp example if relevant.
Key Points (listen for):
Goals First: “Clarify your goal—leads, awareness, loyalty—to avoid creating noise.”
Chatbots: 30% cost cut, 50% faster responses (e.g., JUCO AI).
Personalized Marketing: Mailchimp AI, 20% higher email clicks.
Content: Jasper AI, Grok, 80% faster posts (bakery example).
Sales: AI lead scoring, 15-25% better accuracy.
Add if Needed: “MoilApp used AI for targeted job ads, doubling applicant engagement by focusing on our goal: quality hires.”
Tools: Mailchimp AI, Jasper AI, Grok, ChatGPT—user-friendly for SMBs.
Engage: Josh shares JUCO AI example. (Chime in: “Similar to how MoilApp targets offline workers.”)
Transition: “Great marketing insights, Josh! Now I’ll cover AI for operations.”
5. Practical AI Applications: Operations &amp; Efficiency (8 min, lead)
Goal: Show AI for operations, address SMB resource constraints.
Inventory (2 min): “Predictive AI cuts stockouts 15%. Restaurant saved $5,000/year optimizing stock.”
Financial Analysis (2 min): “QuickBooks AI categorizes expenses in real-time—no finance team needed.”
HR &amp; Recruitment (2 min): “MoilApp’s AI screens resumes, matches candidates, cuts hiring time 30%.”
Supply Chain (2 min): “AI route planning saves 5-10% fuel. Vendor scoring lowers costs.”
SMB Focus: “These tools help lean teams do more with less.”
Tools: QuickBooks AI (financials), MoilApp HR AI (hiring, free 3 weeks), predictive AI (inventory, supply chain).
Engage: “Raise your hand if inventory or hiring eats your time. AI fixes that!”
Transition: “Now let’s get hands-on with AI—starting with actionable steps.”
6. Getting Started with AI: Actionable Steps (8 min, lead)
Goal: Provide roadmap, emphasize shaping AI to fit SMB identity.
Pain Points (2 min): “Start small—pick one repetitive task (scheduling, follow-ups).”
Existing Tools (2 min): “Check Microsoft 365 Copilot in your current software.”
Low-Cost Options (2 min): “ChatGPT, Grok (free on x.com), Canva’s Magic Studio—affordable, easy.”
Pilot Projects (1 min): “Test AI with $500-$2,000 budget, measure in 30 days.”
Shape AI (1 min): “Don’t become what AI gives you—make AI become you. Don’t lose yourself. Question outputs, have conversations, spark debate, dig deeper. Tweak Grok’s blog post to match your brand.”
Tools: Microsoft 365 Copilot, ChatGPT, Grok, Canva’s Magic Studio—accessible for SMBs.
Engage: “What task would you automate? We’ll brainstorm post-demo.”
Transition: “Let’s see AI in action with a live landing page demo!”
7. Live Demo: Landing Page with One Prompt (15 min, lead)
Goal: Show AI’s power, create landing page in 15 min.
Setup (2 min): “We’ll create a coffee shop landing page in 15 min using Grok (free on x.com) or ChatGPT. This shows AI’s speed for SMBs.”
Prompt (1 min): Show on-screen:
“Act as a web designer. Create a landing page outline for a local coffee shop, targeting young professionals. Include headline, subheadline, call-to-action, ‘About Us,’ ‘Menu.’ Modern, friendly tone. Under 300 words, HTML format.”
Explain: “Role: web designer. Task: landing page. Details: coffee shop, young professionals. Tone: friendly. Constraints: 300 words, HTML.”
Demo (10 min):
Input prompt in Grok/ChatGPT live.
Show output (HTML/text).
Copy to CodePen/local editor, display page.
Highlight: “Headline grabs attention, call-to-action drives orders—done in minutes!”
Wrap-Up (2 min): “Tweak this prompt for your business—no coding needed. AI empowers small teams fast.”
Tools: Grok, ChatGPT (demo), CodePen (optional rendering).
Engage: “Volunteer a business type (bakery, gym) to tweak prompt live, if time.”
Transition: “Now, let’s tackle AI challenges to prepare you.”
8. Challenges &amp; Considerations (6 min, lead)
Goal: Address concerns, build trust.
Data Privacy (2 min): “68% worry about AI data. Use GDPR/CCPA-compliant tools like Grok, Anthropic; avoid sensitive data.”
Bias &amp; Ethics (1 min): “AI can have biases. Review outputs, use diverse data for fairness.”
Integration (1 min): “Pick tools like QuickBooks, Shopify that fit your systems.”
Training (2 min): “AI impacts 70% of roles. Upskill one person via Coursera, Udemy.”
SMB Focus: “Challenges are manageable—start small, test carefully.”
Tools: Grok, Anthropic (safe), Coursera, Udemy (training).
Engage: “What’s your biggest AI concern? Save it for Q&amp;A.”
Transition: “Before Q&amp;A, exciting MoilApp news!”
9. MoilApp Announcement: Free Tools (3 min, lead)
Goal: Promote free tools, tie to SMB growth.
“Two free MoilApp tools to grow your business, even with small teams.”
Business Plan Creator: “AI-powered, free for 3 weeks. Create a professional plan in &lt;1 hour for funding, strategy.”
Hiring Platform: “AI-driven, free for 3 weeks. Screens resumes, matches candidates, cuts hiring time 30%.”
Call to Action: “Visit moilapp.com to try both—solve time, resource struggles.”
Tools: MoilApp’s Business Plan Creator, Hiring Platform—free, SMB-focused.
Engage: “Who needs a business plan or hiring help? Free for 3 weeks!”
Transition: “Let’s wrap with Q&amp;A and next steps.”
10. Q&amp;A and Your Next Steps (10 min, co-lead with Joshua)
Goal: Engage, inspire action, reinforce urgency.
Q&amp;A (7 min): “Ask anything—tools, challenges. In 2025, no question goes unanswered!”
Anticipate: “Best tool for my industry?” (Grok, ChatGPT versatile). “Avoid mistakes?” (Review outputs, test small).
Example: “MoilApp used AI for job postings—you can too.”
Next Steps (3 min):
“Test Grok, ChatGPT for 90 days, measure impact.”
“Learn via Coursera, Udemy, AI forums.”
“Try MoilApp’s free tools at moilapp.com.”
“Email hive@budaedc.com for follow-up, AI community.”
Urgency: “Don’t miss AI like we did Google. The sky’s the limit—start today!”
Close: “No big budget needed. Experiment, achieve more. We’re rooting for you!”
Tools: Grok, ChatGPT, MoilApp tools, Coursera, Udemy—accessible, actionable.
Engage: “Commit to one AI tool this week—share your plan at networking!”

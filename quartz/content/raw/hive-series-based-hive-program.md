Series-Based HIVE Program
Hatch Stage (Idea Stage)
Target Audience: Entrepreneurs with ideas but no formal business.Duration: 6 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops, mix of LMS and in-person at Buda HIVE).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Ideation techniques (brainstorming, problem-solution fit).
Week 2: Market research fundamentals (surveys, competitor analysis).
Week 3: Business concept development (value proposition, customer segments).
Week 4: Business planning.
Week 5: Legal and regulatory basics (business structures, permits).
Week 6: Finalize business plan, pitch preparation.
Tools: SBA templates for structure.
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Registered business with a completed business plan.
Initiate Stage (Startup Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses launching or recently launched.Duration: 6 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Product development (prototyping, iterations).
Week 2: Minimum Viable Product (MVP) creation and testing.
Week 3: Customer acquisition (outbound strategies, early adopters).
Week 4: Basic financial management (budgeting, cash flow).
Week 5: Branding and marketing fundamentals (positioning, social media).
Week 6: Review progress, refine strategies, set revenue goals.
Resources: Texas SBDC coaching sessions, subsidized QuickBooks licenses.
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Operational business with initial revenue.
Validate Stage (Established Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses with consistent operations seeking to validate their model.Duration: 4 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Process optimization (lean methodologies, bottleneck identification).
Week 2: CRM implementation (e.g., HubSpot setup, customer data management).
Week 3: Scaling marketing efforts (content marketing, paid ads).
Week 4: Financial forecasting and budgeting (projections, cost management).
Resources: CRM implementation support, marketing strategy templates.
Mentorship: Weekly sessions with mentors experienced in SMB scaling.Milestone: Optimized operations with measurable growth (e.g., 10% revenue increase).
Expand Stage (Scale Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses ready to scale or enter new markets.Duration: 4 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Advanced growth strategies (market expansion, partnerships).
Week 2: Accessing capital (pitch decks, investor expectations).
Week 3: Workforce development (hiring, training programs).
Week 4: Strategic planning (3–5-year roadmap).
Resources: Introductions to Capital Factory investors, workforce training via Texas Woman’s University.
Mentorship: Bi-weekly sessions with growth strategists and investors.Milestone: Scaled operations or successful new market entry (e.g., 20% market share in new region).

# Know Me — Extraction Prompts for The Brain
**Purpose:** Use these prompts in ChatGPT, Claude, or Grok to extract deep personal + professional context about Andres Urrego. Save each output and drop into `~/My Brain/knowledge-base/raw/` for ingestion.

## PROMPT 1 — CLAUDE (Best for structured depth)

Use this in a fresh Claude.ai conversation. Be as specific and honest as possible — vague answers produce vague intelligence.

```
I'm building a personal AI knowledge base called "The Brain" that will act as my second brain and business co-pilot. I need you to interview me comprehensively so the Brain knows me deeply enough to think like me, advise me, and represent me accurately.

Ask me these questions one section at a time, waiting for my full answer before moving on. After I answer all sections, produce a single structured markdown document with everything organized.

SECTION 1 — WHO YOU ARE
1. Full name, age, where you're from originally, where you live now
2. What's your background before Moil? (education, past jobs, industries)
3. What's your native language and how does being bilingual shape how you operate?
4. What are 3 things most people don't know about you that are relevant to how you work?
5. What are your non-negotiable values — the things you won't compromise on?
6. How would your closest collaborators describe your strengths? Your blind spots?

SECTION 2 — MOIL RIGHT NOW
1. What exactly does Moil do, in one sentence you'd say to a stranger at a coffee shop?
2. Who is your current ideal customer — be hyper-specific (not "small businesses," but actual profile: industry, size, location, pain, urgency)
3. What's working right now? What are you seeing that gives you conviction?
4. What's broken or not figured out yet?
5. What's your current revenue? How many active customers? What's the churn situation?
6. What does the fundraise look like — who are you talking to, what's the ask, what's the timeline?
7. Who are the 3 most dangerous competitors right now and why?

SECTION 3 — YOUR VISION
1. What does Moil look like in 3 years if everything goes right?
2. What's the category you're trying to own? What does winning that category look like?
3. What's the insight that most people in your market are missing that you've figured out?
4. Why are you the right person to build this? What's the unfair advantage?

SECTION 4 — HOW YOU THINK AND OPERATE
1. How do you make decisions under uncertainty? Walk me through a recent hard call.
2. What frameworks or mental models do you actually use (not just know about)?
3. What does your typical week look like? Where does your time actually go?
4. What do you read, watch, or listen to that shapes your thinking?
5. Who are your biggest influences — in business, in life?
6. What's a belief you held 2 years ago that you've completely reversed?

SECTION 5 — RELATIONSHIPS AND NETWORK
1. Who are your 3-5 most important professional relationships right now? What makes them important?
2. Who are your advisors and what do you go to each of them for?
3. Who are your most important current customers and what do they love about Moil?
4. Who do you need to meet that you haven't yet?

SECTION 6 — 2026 PRIORITIES
1. What are the 3 things that, if accomplished this year, would make 2026 a success?
2. What's the one thing you're most afraid of in 2026?
3. What decision are you currently stuck on?
4. What do you need more of right now — capital, customers, team, clarity, or time?

After I answer everything, produce a clean markdown document titled "Andres Urrego — Personal Intelligence Brief" that I can drop into my knowledge base.
```

## PROMPT 2 — CHATGPT (Best for conversational extraction)

```
I want you to act as a deep-dive interviewer. Your goal is to produce a comprehensive personal and professional profile of me — Andres Urrego — that an AI system can use to understand me, think like me, and advise me on my business.

This is for my personal AI knowledge base. Ask me 5-6 questions at a time, then synthesize my answers before moving to the next batch. Be relentless about getting specifics — if I'm vague, push back and ask for a concrete example.

Cover these areas in order:

**Round 1: Identity and origin**
- Who are you, where did you come from, what shaped you before Moil?
- What does being Colombian and working in the US tech space mean for how you see opportunities?
- What drives you at a fundamental level — beyond money?

**Round 2: Moil's real status**  
- Exact revenue and customer count right now
- What the product actually does vs. what the website says
- The one thing customers say that makes you feel you're building the right thing
- The one thing that worries you most about the business

**Round 3: Market and competition**
- Who are you actually competing against in the field (not just on paper)
- What do customers use before they find Moil? Why do they switch?
- What's the moat you're building that's hard to copy?

**Round 4: People and capital**
- Who's on the team, what do they own?
- Who are you raising from and what's the pitch?
- Who are the advisors you actually call when things are hard?

**Round 5: Operating style**
- How do you spend your time? What are you doing vs. what should you be doing?
- How do you sell? What's the conversation that converts?
- What tools and systems run your day?

At the end, write a "Moil Context Brief" — a 600-word structured document covering: company status, market position, team, fundraise, competitive landscape, and founder profile. Format in markdown.
```

## PROMPT 3 — GROK (Best for unfiltered insights + X/social context)

```
I need you to help me build a personal intelligence brief about myself — Andres Urrego, founder of Moil (moilapp.com), a workforce management and AI platform for SMBs based in Buda, TX.

I want this to be raw and honest. Push me on things. If something doesn't add up, call it out. The output goes into my personal AI knowledge base so I need it to be true, not polished.

Interview me across these areas:

1. THE UNFILTERED TRUTH ABOUT MOIL
   - What problem are you actually solving vs. what you say you're solving?
   - Where are you pretending the business is further along than it is?
   - What's the thing you haven't admitted to yourself yet about what needs to change?

2. YOUR PERSONAL EDGE
   - What do you understand about your customers that your competitors don't?
   - What have you done that no one else in your market has done?
   - What's the weird or unconventional thing about how you operate that actually works?

3. THE NETWORK AND ECOSYSTEM
   - Who in your network has the most leverage over your outcome right now?
   - What relationships are you underinvesting in?
   - Who needs to hear the Moil story that hasn't yet?

4. CONTENT AND BRAND (since you're building in public)
   - What's your authentic voice online — what do you actually believe that's worth saying?
   - What's the Moil narrative that's most compelling to your audience?
   - What topics do you have real authority on vs. what are you performing authority on?

5. THE NEXT 90 DAYS
   - If you could only do 3 things in the next 90 days, what would they be?
   - What would you do differently if you had $500k in the bank vs. if you had to survive on current revenue?
   - What's the experiment you've been afraid to run?

After the interview, write a 500-word "Founder Intelligence Brief" in markdown. Be direct. Skip the corporate language. Write it the way a trusted advisor who knows everything would describe Andres and Moil to a new investor.
```

## HOW TO USE THESE

1. Pick one (start with Claude — best structured output)
2. Open a fresh conversation in that model
3. Paste the prompt
4. Answer every question honestly and specifically — the more specific, the more useful the Brain becomes
5. At the end, copy the generated markdown document
6. Save it as `andres-intelligence-brief-[date].md`
7. Drop it into `~/My Brain/knowledge-base/raw/`
8. Open Claude Code in `~/My Brain/` and say "ingest the new file in raw/"

The Brain will then build wiki pages for: your profile, Moil context, key relationships, your decision-making patterns, 2026 priorities, and competitive landscape.

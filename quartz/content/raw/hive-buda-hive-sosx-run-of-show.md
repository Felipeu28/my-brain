Buda HIVE SOSX
SOSX Background Information:
This event will be the first to be celebrated during SXSW, during which we collaborate with community partners in our area to support entrepreneurs. This event will help us develop our brand visibility and engage with other stakeholders.
The ROI: New Business leads, Cohort registrants, and subject matter experts. Position the HIVE as a place to gather. Encourage people to explore Buda.
Run of Show
Event Name: SOSXDate: Thursday, March 12, 2026Location: MeridianEvent Time: 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Unofficial: Experience the Vibe of SXSW, Closer to Home
EDC to set up 12:00 pm-2:30 pmEDC Staff will arrive. They will set up the HIVE letters on stage, banners on stage, tickets for drinks, a swag and registration table, and name tags.
5:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Registration &amp; NetworkingThe registration as attendees arrive, just inside the entrance to the Buck’s.
Attendees check in, get one drink ticket, stickers, and network informally with the entrepreneur tables for the Buda-ful Fest portion of the night.
6:00PM – 6:10PM Intro of the EDC: Jacquie Martinez and Casey Early kick it off, welcoming everyone to the event and sharing the vision of the 2nd BFF and SoSX event. They provide an overview of the EDC and how the HIVE plays an important role in our local entrepreneurs and in building the small business ecosystem. Remind attendees that they too can register to participate in the next cohort (cohort 4) which starts in APRIL! (APRIL 20-JUNE 8)
6:10PM - 6:15PM – Jacquie introduces Jennifer Storm, CEO &amp; IEDC Economic Developer of the Year to speak about the Buda EDC and how HIVE, BFF, and staff are making an impact in Buda.
6:15PM - 6:25PM -  Jennifer introduces Incubator Strategists, Josh Edmond &amp; Andres Urrego.
6:25PM - 6:40PM- Josh &amp; Andres come to the microphone and speak about the program and experiences.  They introduce 4 past cohort graduates, one by one.  Bring them up one by one to give a testimonial of their experience and how winning last year’s pitch competition impacted their business.

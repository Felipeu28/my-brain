## Technical Notes

- **Root cause of first attempt failure:** `m365 request ... > file` pipes through UTF-8 text mode, corrupting binary .docx (ZIP) files. The bytes `ef bf bd` (UTF-8 replacement char U+FFFD) were injected throughout the binary.
- **Solution:** Use `m365 util accesstoken get` to get the Bearer token, then `curl -s -L -H "Authorization: Bearer $TOKEN" <url> -o file.docx` for binary-safe download.
- **Recordings/ folder:** All 43 "Meeting Transcript.mp4" files are real H.264 MP4 video files (Teams screen capture at low bitrate). They are NOT text transcripts. Verified with `file` command and hex inspection. No subtitle/VTT tracks embedded.

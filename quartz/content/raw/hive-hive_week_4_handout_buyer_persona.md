HIVE FOUNDATIONS
Week 4: Understanding the Buyer Persona
Buda HIVE | 8-Week Foundations Program
What Is a Buyer Persona?
A buyer persona is a semi-fictional character representing your ideal customer, based on real research and data. It is not a vague target market - it is a specific, detailed person!
Why Personas Matter:
71% of companies exceeding revenue goals have documented personas
73% higher conversion rates with persona-based marketing
6x engagement increase with personalized content
NOT a Persona:
"Our target market is Buda families ages 30-45"
IS a Persona:
"Sarah, 34, marketing director, lives in Buda. Works from home 3 days/week. Struggles with work-life balance. Values convenience + local. Shops at HEB, uses Instagram daily."

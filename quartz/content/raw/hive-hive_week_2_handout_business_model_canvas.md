Week 2: Business Model Canvas
Buda HIVE | 8-Week Foundations Program
What Is the Business Model Canvas?
The Business Model Canvas (BMC) is a one-page strategic tool that breaks down your business into 9 interconnected building blocks. Instead of writing a 40-page business plan, you can map your entire business on ONE page!
Why Use the BMC?
Visual - See your entire business at a glance
Fast - Draft in hours, not weeks
Flexible - Easy to test and pivot ideas
Proven - Startups using BMC are 60% more likely to hit growth targets
Key Stat:
Startups using Business Model Canvas are 60% more likely to hit their growth targets!

The 9 Building Blocks
Your canvas has 9 components that work together:
1. Customer Segments
Who are you serving?
2. Value Propositions
What problem do you solve?
3. Channels
How do customers find and buy from you?
4. Customer Relationships
How do you keep customers happy?
5. Revenue Streams
How do you make money?
6. Key Resources
What assets do you need?
7. Key Activities
What must you do daily?
8. Key Partnerships
Who helps you succeed?
9. Cost Structure
What does it cost to run?

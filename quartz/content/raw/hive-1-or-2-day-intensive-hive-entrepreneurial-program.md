1- or 2-Day Intensive HIVE Entrepreneurial Program
Hatch Stage (Idea Stage)
Target Audience: Entrepreneurs with ideas but no formal business.Duration: 1 day (8 hours, in-person at Buda HIVE).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Morning (4 hours): Ideation (problem-solution fit, value proposition), market research basics (survey tools, competitor analysis).
Afternoon (4 hours): Business planning (key sections: executive summary, market analysis), legal basics (business registration, permits).
Tools: SBA checklist.
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Draft business plan and registered business (or clear registration plan).
Initiate Stage (Startup Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses launching or recently launched.Duration: 1 day (8 hours).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Morning (4 hours): Product development (MVP framework), customer acquisition (outbound tactics, social media).
Afternoon (4 hours): Financial basics (QuickBooks setup, budgeting), branding essentials (logo, messaging).
Resources: Texas SBDC resource guide, QuickBooks trial access.
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Operational business with initial customer outreach plan.
Validate Stage (Established Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses with consistent operations seeking to validate their model.Duration: 2 days (16 hours).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Day 1: Process optimization (lean tools, workflow mapping), CRM basics (HubSpot demo, data setup).
Day 2: Marketing scaling (SEO, paid ads), financial forecasting (Excel templates, projections).
Resources: CRM trial access, marketing playbook.
Mentorship: 2-hour group session with SMB scaling mentors.Milestone: Implemented process improvement and measurable growth plan (e.g., 5% efficiency gain).
Expand Stage (Scale Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses ready to scale or enter new markets.Duration: 2 days (16 hours).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Day 1: Growth strategies (market entry, partnerships), capital access (pitch deck workshop).
Day 2: Workforce planning (hiring frameworks), strategic planning (roadmap creation).
Resources: Capital Factory pitch template, Texas Woman’s University training overview.
Mentorship: 2-hour session with growth strategists and investors.Milestone: Investor-ready pitch deck and scaling roadmap.

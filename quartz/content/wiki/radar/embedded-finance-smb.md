# Embedded Finance for SMBs

**Type:** Radar log — fintech / product strategy
**Last updated:** 2026-04-12
**Related:** [[wiki/clients/warehouse-logistics]], [[wiki/radar/latam-job-market]], [[wiki/concepts/two-sided-marketplace]]

## What This Tracks

Embedded payroll, payments, earned wage access, and financial products being built into SMB software platforms. Relevant as a potential Moil revenue expansion path once hiring is established.

## Why It Matters for Moil

Moil starts with hiring. But the natural next product for an employer of blue-collar workers is payroll, time tracking, and earned wage access. Watching this space now shapes when/whether to expand.

## Key Players (2025–2026)

| Company | Product | Stage | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|-------|
| **Check** | Embedded payroll API | Growth | Powers payroll in 50+ vertical SaaS products |
| **Gusto Embedded** | Payroll API | Growth | Gusto's B2B2C play |
| **Pinwheel** | Earned wage access API | Growth | Connects payroll data to financial apps |
| **DailyPay / Branch** | Earned wage access | Scale | Used by Walmart, McDonald's, warehouse operators |
| **Deel** | Global payroll + compliance | Scale | Heavy focus on contractors and international |
| **Homebase** | Scheduling + payroll for hourly | Growth | Direct competitor in adjacent space |
| **Workstream** | Hiring + onboarding for hourly | Growth | Most similar to Moil; no embedded finance yet |

## Opportunity for Moil

Once Moil has 500+ employer accounts:
1. **Time tracking add-on** — workers clock in/out via WhatsApp/voice
2. **Payroll integration** — plug into Check or Gusto Embedded; Moil becomes the payroll UX layer
3. **Earned wage access** — workers can request early pay through Moil app; Pinwheel or DailyPay provides rails
4. **Revenue model**: 0.5–1% of payroll processed; enormous revenue expansion vs. $15–$75/mo SaaS

## Risk
- Payroll compliance is complex; undercapitalized startups get burned
- Non-core distraction if Moil is still finding PMF on hiring
- Watch what Homebase and Workstream do first

# AI-Native Org Design

## Summary
The emerging body of thinking about how companies should be structured when AI can perform most knowledge-worker tasks autonomously. The dominant essay in the bookmark period is Jack Dorsey's "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" (Sequoia, 5.6M views) — arguing that the old org chart (hierarchical, role-based) is being replaced by networks of AI agents with human oversight at the edges. A second signal: @osf_rekt's "I gave Claude a brain and now it runs my company" — Claude replaced $400K worth of roles at @RektDrinks.

## Key Ideas

### From Hierarchy to Intelligence (Jack Dorsey / Sequoia)
- Traditional orgs: hierarchy exists because humans need coordination overhead
- AI-native orgs: intelligence is distributed across agents that coordinate directly; hierarchy is overhead without function
- The winning structure: small human team setting direction + large swarm of AI agents executing
- Speed advantage: AI agents don't need meetings, don't have context-switching cost, don't get tired
- Source: @jack, 5.6M views, Apr 2, 2026

### The "$400K Replaced" Pattern
- @osf_rekt: "Claude replaced $400K worth of roles at RektDrinks" — structured agent workflows replaced hiring for specific functions
- Key roles most frequently replaced by AI in SMB: content creator, data analyst, email marketer, customer support tier 1, ops coordinator
- This is the use case Moil's AI Co-Founder pitch is targeting

### The "7 Text Files" Meme (@agentskills_ai)
- "These 7 text files will run your business in 2027"
- The minimum runnable AI company: AGENTS.md + MEMORY.md + SOUL.md + DREAMS.md + skills/ + context/ + workflow triggers
- Not far from what the Moil Brain already does

### The Marketer-as-Engineer Crossover (@gregisenberg)
- "The best thing any engineer/programmer can do right now is learn to become a top 1% marketer"
- The reverse is also true: the best marketers are learning to code agents
- SMB owners who master this crossover become dramatically more competitive

## Current State (April 2026)

AI video generation hit a major inflection point. Key developments from bookmarks:

- **Seedance 2.0** launched on Higgsfield — joint audio-video generation. Available without restrictions on fal.ai. Multiple posts totaling 6M+ views.
- **HeyGen** — "We solved character consistency. Forever Avatar V" — major for brand content.
- **Google Veo 3.1 Lite** — $0.05/sec at 720p. Google Vids now includes Veo 3.1 + Lyria 3 in Workspace. Free video generation.
- **Grok Imagine** — video generation from xAI/Elon Musk.

## Why This Matters for Moil

[[Content360]] already includes AI video for highest-impact days (5+ video days on Market Pro). As AI video costs drop ($0.05/sec from Google), Moil could:

1. Increase video credits across all tiers at minimal cost
2. Offer character-consistent brand videos (HeyGen pattern)
3. Position Content360 as a full video production department, not just images + captions

The @sammgrowth post — "How to make $10k with TikTok using AI characters" — is the exact use case Moil's SMB customers need: short-form video content for social without hiring a videographer.

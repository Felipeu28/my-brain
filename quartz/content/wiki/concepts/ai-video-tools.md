## Current State (April 2026)

AI video generation hit a major inflection point. Key developments from bookmarks:

- **Seedance 2.0** launched on Higgsfield — joint audio-video generation. Available without restrictions on fal.ai. Multiple posts totaling 6M+ views.
- **HeyGen** — "We solved character consistency. Forever Avatar V" — major for brand content.
- **Google Veo 3.1 Lite** — $0.05/sec at 720p. Google Vids now includes Veo 3.1 + Lyria 3 in Workspace. Free video generation.
- **Grok Imagine** — video generation from xAI/Elon Musk.

## The "$1M/mo AI Video Team" Workflow (@VibeMarketer_, Apr 9)
The structure of high-performing AI video teams:
1. Script → Claude
2. Voiceover → Eleven Labs
3. Visuals/motion → Seedance 2.0 or Veo 3.1
4. Character/avatar → HeyGen Avatar V
5. Editing → CapCut or DaVinci Resolve
6. Distribution → TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

Cost: near zero per video once the workflow is set up. Revenue: @sammgrowth claims $10k/mo TikTok from AI characters; @AmirMushich lists 50 brands paying for this format.

## Why This Matters for Moil

[[wiki/concepts/content360|Content360]] already includes AI video for highest-impact days (5+ video days on Market Pro). As AI video costs drop ($0.05/sec from Google), Moil could:

1. Increase video credits across all tiers at minimal cost
2. Offer character-consistent brand videos (HeyGen Avatar V pattern — see [[wiki/concepts/heygen]])
3. Position Content360 as a full video production department, not just images + captions
4. Use Seedance 2.0 (see [[wiki/concepts/seedance]]) for motion content at fal.ai pricing

The @sammgrowth post — "How to make $10k with TikTok using AI characters" — is the exact use case Moil's SMB customers need: short-form video content for social without hiring a videographer.

**Near-term test:** Run one Moil customer's social content through the full stack (Claude script → Seedance visuals → HeyGen avatar) and measure engagement vs. their current static posts.

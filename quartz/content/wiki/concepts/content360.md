Also called **Moil360** on social media.

## What It Is

Content360 is [[wiki/moil/positioning|Moil]]'s AI-powered 30-day marketing engine. After answering the 21 strategic questions, the platform generates a complete monthly content department:

- 30 days of researched topics
- Tested hooks and full captions by content type
- Calls-to-action (CTAs) optimized per post
- Hashtags optimized for each post
- 30 custom AI-generated images (brand-aligned, not stock)
- AI video for highest-impact days (5+ video days included)
- Available in English and Spanish with one-click translation

## Which Plans Include It

- **Starter ($15/mo):** Not included
- **Professional ($25/mo):** Not included
- **Market Pro ($75/mo):** Full Content360 access + 15 video credits/month

Content360 is the primary upsell driver from Professional → Market Pro.

## Competitive Position

No direct competitor offers this as an integrated package:
- Hootsuite/Buffer do scheduling, not content creation
- ChatGPT/Canva can generate pieces but nothing integrated to business DNA
- Traditional agencies charge $2,000–$5,000/month for comparable output

Content360 is the feature that makes Moil more than a hiring tool — it's what makes the "AI co-founder" positioning real. A business owner answers 21 questions once and gets a full marketing department's output.

## Social Media Usage

Andres promotes Content360 as "Moil360" on the MoilWorks Facebook page. Hashtags: #contentmarketing #smallbusiness #entrepreneur #community #Moil360. The marketing angle is "conquer the content beast" — positioning against the time and creative energy small business owners don't have.

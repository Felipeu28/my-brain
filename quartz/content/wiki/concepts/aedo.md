# AEDO — Accredited Economic Development Organization

**Type:** concept
**Last updated:** 2026-04-11
**Source:** [[raw/buda-hive-edc-2026-04-11]]
**Related:** [[wiki/people/jennifer-storm]], [[wiki/concepts/buda-hive]]

---

**Issuing body:** International Economic Development Council (IEDC)
**Significance:** Only 90 organizations worldwide and 12 in Texas hold this accreditation.

## What It Is

AEDO validates professional excellence, organizational structure, and effectiveness through a peer-reviewed evaluation. It's the gold standard credential for economic development corporations.

## Relevance to Moil

[[wiki/orgs/buda-edc|Buda EDC]] ([[wiki/people/jennifer-storm|Jennifer Storm]]) is pursuing AEDO accreditation. Andres wrote a community support letter on Moil letterhead — submitted April 10, 2026, approved by Jennifer. Deadline was April 15.

If Buda EDC earns AEDO, it elevates the credibility of every program they run — including the [[wiki/concepts/buda-hive|Buda HIVE]], which means the Moil partnership gains institutional weight.

## Key Frameworks

### The New LinkedIn Algorithm (Logan Gott, 680K views — Apr 4)
@LoganTGott's guide to beating the 2026 LinkedIn algorithm:
1. **Lead with a hook** — first line must create a pattern interrupt. Questions, stats, counterintuitive claims
2. **Short paragraphs** — 1-3 lines max. Algorithm rewards scroll time; dense text tanks it
3. **No external links in post body** — LinkedIn suppresses posts that drive traffic off-platform. Drop links in comments
4. **Native video is heavily boosted** — upload directly to LinkedIn, not YouTube links
5. **Engage immediately after posting** — reply to every comment in the first hour; algorithm reads this as "hot post"
6. **Document posts outperform opinion posts** — showing a process/result beats sharing an idea
7. **Consistent posting window** — same time, 4+ days/week; algorithm rewards consistency over volume
8. **Use polls strategically** — polls get 5x the reach of normal posts; use to build audience, follow up with content
9. **Tag sparingly** — 1-2 relevant mentions max; more than that reads as spam
10. **Personal profile > company page** — people follow people, not logos. Andres's profile should be primary

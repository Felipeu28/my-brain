## Summary
HeyGen is an AI video platform best known for Avatar V — their character-consistent AI avatar technology announced in April 2026. The "we solved character consistency forever" claim is significant because inconsistent character appearance was the #1 friction in branded AI video. HeyGen's Avatar V captures someone in 15 seconds and maintains that identity across all future videos.

## Key Points
- **Avatar V** — character captured in 15 seconds, consistent across all future content
- Official announcement: @HeyGen Apr 8, 2026 — video post, significant engagement
- **What "character consistency" solves:** SMBs could have a consistent AI brand spokesperson without hiring talent or doing retakes. One capture session → infinite branded video.
- **Competitive significance:** This closes the gap between AI video and professional production for brand identity use cases.

## Why It Matters for Content Marketing
Character consistency was the last major objection brands had to AI video ("but it never looks like us"). HeyGen has eliminated that. This moves AI video from "experimental" to "default choice" for social content at SMB scale.

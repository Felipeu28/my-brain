## Concept

An AI platform for small political campaigns. Core premise: small campaigns can't afford the data tools large campaigns use, and races are often won/lost by single digits.

## Pain Points Identified

- Demographic and voter research
- Graphic design (signs, logos, websites, donation platforms)
- Local event calendars and community relations

## Name Origin

Brainstormed with AI — Jennifer liked the "operating system" metaphor.

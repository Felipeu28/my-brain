## Key Decisions — Prompt Engineering

- **Goal:** Achieve a comprehensive, 4-page business plan with real company data from minimal user input
- Process: Andres and team iterated prompts live, testing with ChatGPT and DeepSeek
- **Final result:** Revised prompt generates 4-page business plan with real company data — team satisfied
- DeepSeek uses significantly more tokens than OpenAI for similar output → **switching to OpenAI** for now
- **GPT-4 Turbo** too expensive for marginal benefit → use standard **GPT-4**
- Temperature parameter discussed for controlling AI "creativity level"
- "Deep research" function in AI: AI generates further questions and self-answers → much more comprehensive but very slow (time-intensive)
- **Business Creator Personas** idea proposed: named personas ("Bougie Betty," "Health-Conscious Mary") for marketing + relatable content targeting

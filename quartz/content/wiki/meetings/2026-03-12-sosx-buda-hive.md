## Summary

SoSX (parallel-to-SXSW) event hosted by [[wiki/orgs/buda-edc|Buda EDC]] at [[wiki/orgs/meridian-buda|Meridian Buda]] on **March 12, 2026**. The biggest public event of the Moil–Buda HIVE relationship — Cohorts 2+3 received certificates, HIVE alumni pitched in a competition format, and **Andres was publicly named the Moil Business Coach and Incubator Strategist** in front of Buda's business community.

This is the single most important status milestone for Andres's public role with Buda EDC before the Apr 15 Board vote.

## Key Moments

- **Certificates:** Cohorts 2 + 3 received formal completion certificates
- **Pitch competition:** HIVE alumni pitched; Buda community + EDC leadership judged
- **Andres publicly named:** Moil Business Coach + Incubator Strategist — first public recognition of the institutional role
- **Venue synergy:** Meridian Buda (Travis Sutherland's Moil-client venue) hosted; reinforces the local-ecosystem story

## Why It Matters

1. **Public credibility** — Andres's role was private/contractual before SoSX; after SoSX it's on the record with the community
2. **Referenceability** — useful for the Helotes EDC pitch (mirror model) and other EDC prospects
3. **Buda EDC board momentum** — shapes the Apr 15 Board vote on the Incubator Strategist contract
4. **Cohort 4 launch setup** — cohort members watching cohorts 2+3 graduate sets expectations

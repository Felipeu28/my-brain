## What this folder is

One page per meeting, discussion thread, or asynchronous decision thread that was processed from a raw source. Meeting pages are the action-item extraction layer — they connect who decided what, when, and why.

## Format convention

Each page follows:
- **Date** — when the meeting or thread occurred
- **Participants** — who was involved
- **Channel / Medium** — where it happened (Teams, Zoom, iMessage, email)
- **Key decisions** — what was decided (not just discussed)
- **Action items** — owner + deadline for each
- **Follow-ups needed** — open questions

## What Was Discussed

1. **Moil's blue-collar mission** — 70% of US workers in non-white-collar jobs have never used a digital tool to be hired; Moil bridges that gap
2. **ATS filters** — 7 out of 10 resumes rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems due to missing keywords; Moil's AI generates keyword-rich resumes from voice/text input
3. **Resume builder demo** — Andres walked Rick through the product; Rick loved it and planned to create his own resume that day
4. **CTE practicum students** — Hays CISD seniors go out into the workforce ("practicums") and need job application skills; Rick sees Moil as ideal for this cohort
5. **August 2025 Professional Development** — Hays CISD holds PD workshop sessions for CTE teachers in early August; Rick wants to include Moil as a 45-minute session; Andres would present directly to teachers
6. **Free for candidates** — Rick confirmed he appreciates the model: free for workers, paid by businesses
7. **Moil metrics shared** — 5,000+ candidates, 7,000+ jobs, 100+ companies, all organic growth
8. **Nonprofit angle** — Andres mentioned his nonprofit (separate from Moil for-profit) that handles free workshops; this removes friction for schools who can't receive commercial services

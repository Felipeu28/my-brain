## Summary

Long working session entirely dedicated to designing the Business Plan Creator workflow and template structure. The team debated approaches, reviewed multiple external templates, and reached consensus on an MVP-style workflow and content structure.

---

## Key Decisions — Workflow

**Final agreed workflow:**
1. Single open-ended question: "Tell me about your business idea"
2. AI generates a complete draft business plan from that input
3. User edits section by section (pre-built sections with boxes)
4. Final preview screen

- Design uses existing section boxes (not raw AI text dump)
- Preview shows structured document, NOT raw ChatGPT output

---

## Key Decisions — Template Structure (MVP)

Agreed sections for the MVP business plan:
- Executive Summary (business name, concept, vision, mission, problem statement, solution, target market, revenue model, financial highlights)
- Company Overview
- Problem & Solution
- Target Market
- Competition
- Marketing Plan
- Financial Projections (simplified)

**Philosophy:** Concise MVP for startups and new businesses, NOT an exhaustive master plan.

---

## Key Decisions — Technology

- Tool choice for AI generation: ChatGPT preferred (no firm lock-in yet)
- PDF and Word export formats planned
- Tables to be supported in final output

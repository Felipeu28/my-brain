Pick one. Working hypothesis after the call: **elderly-adult tech autonomy** (scams + bills + medication + password resets).
2. **Validate before building.** Do **5–10 in-person interviews** with elderly adults first. Use the [[wiki/orgs/buda-edc|Buda EDC]] cohort framing as the credibility wedge: *"I'm part of the Buda Economic Development Corporation cohort, building a tool to help seniors regain tech autonomy."*
3. **Competitor reference defines the price ceiling.** Senior Tech Navigators tops out at **$120/mo** with 4 in-person visits/yr. Anyone using them already pays $1,440/yr. A $50/mo ($600/yr) digital alternative at half the perceived value is viable.
4. **Partner with the middlemen.** Senior-tech orgs already have the data Jordan needs and already work with the ICP. Pitch them once you have an MVP: *"relieve some of the teaching workload — buy 10 licenses to distribute to the seniors you serve."*
5. **The "diagnostics visit" pattern.** Sit in a room with 5–10 seniors, have them show you on their phones where it hurts, raise-hand which pain points (bills, medication, scams) matter most. Don't rely on his mother filtering the questions remotely — go to Port Arthur in person.
6. **Cohort license signal (not yet confirmed).** Andres told Jordan he's "pretty sure" all Cohort 4 members will get a full-year Moil business license via the EDC, expected to finalize **this week**. *Second independent surfacing of this precedent — first was the confidential disclosure to Caro on 2026-05-12 ([[wiki/meetings/2026-05-12-carolina-coaching]]).* Still gated on EDC confirmation.
7. **Cohort name-drop is free institutional validation.** *"Most cohort members underuse the EDC affiliation. From a city standpoint, there's no validation that weighs more than that."* Andres encouraged Jordan to lead every outreach with the Buda EDC framing.
8. **Moil shipped a silent feature: 3 emails/week proactive coaching.** Mondays-and-throughout-week emails to active business users summarize pending items, surface challenges, act *"more agentic."* **Pushed silently, not yet announced.** Pulled directly from the [[wiki/projects/clio|Clio]] model. *First time this feature has been documented in the Brain.*

## Key Points

### Echo Squad (LinkedIn Engagement Pod)
- Small group (<10 people) formed from Leadership Boston alumni
- **Cadence:** Every Thursday, 9-10 AM
- **Process:** Post your LinkedIn content, drop the link in the group chat. Everyone likes and comments on each other's posts
- **Results:** Monica saw impressions quadruple — single posts going from ~200 to 1,000+ impressions
- **Rules:** Must post link (not screenshot); like AND comment (not just like); do it in the 9-10 AM window for algorithm benefit
- **Duration:** Through February 28 (3 months)
- **Platform:** LinkedIn only for now; Instagram/Facebook to be added later once rhythm is established
- Andres already seeing results: 9,050 impression growth day-over-day after one post (Nov 24)

## Context

Andres confronted the marketing team about **flatlined results** — same content cadence for months, no sales attribution, no following growth, no Spanish content (was 20 posts/week, now 0). Framed the team as *"the bottleneck"* and called for a **hard reset around research, KPIs, and a Friday → Monday delivery cadence** before any new content is produced. This is the **first time Andres has put a formal marketing reset on the record** — the Apr 24 *"this close to closing our full marketing department"* (1:1 to Jacob) and the Apr 24 38-message AI-adoption clash with Ablad were the precursors; today is the structured rebuild instead of the threat.

## Key decisions

1. **Pause new content production** until baseline research is complete. No more Photoshop/Adobe assets until the team can articulate what's working in the market and why.
2. **Move to research-driven, data-driven decisions.** Every piece of content must answer *"what is this for?"* before it is created.
3. **New weekly cadence — Friday results-and-planning call.**
   - Friday = Ablad delivers content for the *following* week; team reviews previous week's results; revisions Sat/Sun; posts go live Monday.
   - Monday team call stays focused on that week's delivery, not strategy.
   - First call: **Fri 2026-05-22**.
4. **Track concrete KPIs** — likes, comments, organic shares (team-generated shares don't count), and manual invitations sent to try Moil. **Jacob owns building the tracking process.**
5. **Manual outbound as a measurable activity.** Jacob personally DMs/comments to invite people to try Moil (target: **26–40/month**). If the manual loop works, Moil hires someone dedicated to it. Softer baseline framing of the May 11 *"10 real DM conversations/day"* rule (200+/month if hit perfectly) — this is the floor, not the ceiling.
6. **Reference companies the team must study**: Claude, ChatGPT, Y Combinator launch/demo videos, Motion. Andres specifically called out *hyperframes by Hai Jen* — a reference he sent Ablad *"a couple weeks ago"* that still hasn't been acted on. Claude itself uses hyperframes for promo videos.
7. **Restart Spanish content (implicit).** Andres flagged that Moil used to publish ~20 Spanish posts/week and *"someone made the decision and we just stopped."* He wants to know what changed.

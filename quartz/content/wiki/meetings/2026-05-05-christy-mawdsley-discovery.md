## Key decisions

1. **No paid ads yet.** Wait until Moil 21-question intake completes the persona work. With a 45–75-women audience and zero account history, ad dollars currently reach the wrong audience (incl. outside the country)
2. **Facebook over Instagram for the 45–75 segment** — that demographic lives in Facebook groups; Instagram remains brand-awareness only
3. **Education-themed monthly Moil 360 strategy** — Christy's patients are "wide-eyed" when she explains things in session; community + connection beats promotional
4. **Referral program is her highest-leverage near-term move** — given that nearly all current patients come from Pilates instructors, an Austin chain of yoga studios, and physicians
5. **Image strategy:** replace the stock-photo "young models" on the site (added by a Philippines VA) with natural photos of Christy herself — reinforces Roxanna's prior advice

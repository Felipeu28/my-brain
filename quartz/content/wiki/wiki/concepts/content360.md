# Content360 (deprecated → Moil360)

**Type:** deprecated alias
**Last updated:** 2026-04-14
**Canonical:** [[wiki/concepts/moil360]]

---

> **Deprecated as of 2026-04-14.** "Content360" was the internal feature name for what is customer-facing as **Moil360**. To reduce confusion, the canonical product name going forward is **Moil360**. This page exists to catch historical references and redirect.

**Go to → [[wiki/concepts/moil360]]**

## Why the merge

- Customer-facing marketing, social copy, and pipeline all used "Moil 360" / "Moil360"
- Internal product docs called the same feature "Content360"
- Two names for one thing caused confusion in Teams, pricing, and customer conversations
- April 2026 audit forced a single-name decision → Moil360 wins (it's the customer-facing name)

## Historical mentions

If you're reading an old document that says "Content360", mentally substitute "Moil360" — they are the same product. The 30-day marketing engine included in Market Pro ($75/mo).

See [[wiki/concepts/moil360]] for current product documentation.

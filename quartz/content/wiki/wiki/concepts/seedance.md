## Summary
Seedance 2.0 is ByteDance's AI video generation model, notable for joint audio-video synthesis. It launched on Higgsfield and fal.ai in late March/early April 2026 and generated an immediate wave of creator content. Multiple X posts totaling 6M+ views in the bookmark window. It's one of the clearest signals that AI video has moved from novelty to production-ready tool.

## Key Points
- **Joint audio-video** — Seedance 2.0 generates synchronized audio and video in one pass, unlike prior tools that needed separate audio layering
- **Distribution:** Available on Higgsfield (with 65% OFF promo) and fal.ai (unrestricted access, no waitlist)
- **View counts:** Apr 1 Higgsfield post — 4.8M views; Apr 9 launch — 1.3M views
- **Creator wave:** @AmirMushich: "50 places brands are paying for video already — most AI creators walk right past them"
- **$10k TikTok pattern:** @sammgrowth: AI characters + Seedance = scalable faceless content income

## Where to Access

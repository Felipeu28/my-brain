## What It Is

The HIVE Program is the structured entrepreneurship curriculum run by [[wiki/orgs/buda-edc|Buda EDC]] through the [[wiki/concepts/buda-hive|Buda HIVE]] incubator. It uses a **stage-gated, series-based model** — entrepreneurs enter at the stage that matches where their business actually is, then progress through the program.

Andres Urrego and [[wiki/people/joshua-edmond|Josh Edmond]] serve as contracted **Incubator Strategists** who design and deliver this curriculum.

## Four Stages

### Stage 1: Hatch (Idea Stage)
**Target:** Entrepreneurs with ideas but no formal business
**Duration:** 6 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops, mix of LMS + in-person)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Ideation techniques — brainstorming, problem-solution fit |
| 2 | Market research fundamentals — surveys, competitor analysis |
| 3 | Business concept development — value proposition, customer segments |
| 4 | Business planning |
| 5 | Legal & regulatory basics — business structures, permits |
| 6 | Finalize business plan + pitch preparation |

**Tools:** SBA templates
**Mentorship:** 1:1 coaching
**Milestone:** Registered business with a completed business plan

---

### Stage 2: Initiate (Startup Stage)
**Target:** Businesses launching or recently launched
**Duration:** 6 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Product development — prototyping, iteration |
| 2 | MVP creation and testing |
| 3 | Customer acquisition — outbound strategies, early adopters |
| 4 | Basic financial management — budgeting, cash flow |
| 5 | Branding & marketing fundamentals — positioning, social media |
| 6 | Review progress, set revenue goals |

**Resources:** Texas SBDC coaching sessions, subsidized QuickBooks licenses
**Mentorship:** 1:1 coaching
**Milestone:** Operational business with initial revenue

---

### Stage 3: Validate (Established Stage)
**Target:** Businesses with consistent operations seeking to validate their model
**Duration:** 4 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Process optimization — lean methodologies, bottleneck identification |
| 2 | CRM implementation — HubSpot setup, customer data management |
| 3 | Scaling marketing — content marketing, paid ads |
| 4 | Financial forecasting and budgeting |

**Resources:** CRM implementation support, marketing strategy templates
**Mentorship:** Weekly sessions with SMB scaling mentors
**Milestone:** Optimized operations with measurable growth (10% revenue increase)

---

### Stage 4: Expand (Scale Stage)
**Target:** Businesses ready to scale or enter new markets
**Duration:** 4 weeks (weekly 2-hour workshops)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Advanced growth strategies — market expansion, partnerships |
| 2 | Accessing capital — pitch decks, investor expectations |
| 3 | Workforce development — hiring, training |
| 4 | Strategic planning — 3–5-year roadmap |

**Resources:** Introductions to Capital Factory investors; workforce training via Texas Woman's University
**Mentorship:** Bi-weekly sessions with growth strategists and investors
**Milestone:** Scaled operations or new market entry (20% market share in new region)

## Moil Integration

[[wiki/moil/positioning|Moil]] platform licenses are issued to each HIVE cohort participant as part of the program. Entrepreneurs learn to use Moil during Stage 2–3 (business planning, CRM, marketing tools). This creates loyal users who continue after graduation.

**Moil Business Plan Creator** is specifically recommended to HIVE clients in coaching sessions as a tool for brand clarity and business structure.

## Program Format Variants

The HIVE program has been designed in multiple delivery formats to fit different community needs:

| Format | Duration | Setting | Use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Series-based** (primary) | 6-week + 4-week stages | Weekly 2-hr workshops, LMS + in-person | Standard cohort delivery |
| **Intensive** | 1–2 days (8 hrs each) | In-person at Buda HIVE | Compressed workshops for time-constrained participants |
| **Hybrid** | 6 weeks per stage | LMS + in-person mix | Flexible attendance |

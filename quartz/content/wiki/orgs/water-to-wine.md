## Why this org has a wiki page

Water To Wine is the **direct competitor** to the wine concept Christine St John + Kat Eyers are building inside HIVE Cohort 4. Despite the friendly framing of their Apr 30 introductory meeting (Dane was unusually open and gave them a generous Q&A), Andres flagged Dane as the fiercest competitor in Buda for the same wine-buying customer base. Christine was a wine-club member at Water To Wine; Andres pushed her to cancel during the May 4 coaching session.

## Strategic angle: custom-label partnership

Dane offered Christine + Kat the option to use Water To Wine's labeling service so they can launch *community-named / family-named custom-label wines* without buying fermentation equipment. Andres greenlit this as a differentiation pillar — *"the back of the wine bottle would just say water to wine"* — first vendor relationship in the wine concept's plan.

## What It Is

Echo Squad (formerly "Reciprocate Pod") is a LinkedIn engagement pod organized by [[wiki/people/monica-pena|Monica Pena]] of EGBI. It's a small group (<10 people) that mutually likes and comments on each other's LinkedIn posts on a fixed schedule to amplify reach through the algorithm.

**Mechanism:** Members share their LinkedIn post link in a group chat every Thursday 9–10 AM. Others open all links, like + comment, and close. The process takes ~10 minutes per week.

**Algorithm insight from Monica:** LinkedIn's algorithm rewards engagement speed and consistency. Members who post once/week see 4x more profile views. The 9–10 AM window is optimal. Monica's impressions went from ~200 per post to 1,000+ after joining.

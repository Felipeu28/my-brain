## Summary
Formal business plan dated March 5, 2026. HQ: Buda, TX. Mission: affordable, AI-driven SMB tools. 500+ active businesses, $72K–96K ARR, targeting $300K–400K by end of 2027 and $750K–1M by 2028. 81.7% internal viability ("Moil Score").

## Key Points

### Traction (Mar 2026)
- 500+ active businesses
- 5,000+ jobs posted monthly
- 94% interview success rate
- $850 avg cost per hire (vs $2,400 industry)
- 91% 90-day retention
- 58% more bilingual candidate reach than industry avg

### Pricing
- Starter $15/mo · Professional $25/mo · Market Pro $75/mo · 30-day money-back · SOC 2.

### Financial projections (3-yr)
- **2026:** $72–96K ARR · OpEx $300K · net ≈ −$225K
- **2027:** $300–400K ARR · OpEx $400K · net ≈ −$140K
- **2028:** $750K–1M ARR · OpEx $500K · net +$60–250K (break-even Q2 2027)

### Core offerings
- 20–30pg market research from 21 strategic questions
- Investor-ready business plan (1-click, 5-yr projections)
- Content360 — 30-day AI marketing engine
- Smart Hiring — 10+ platform auto-post + 95% AI matching
- 24/7 AI Business Coach
- Document Creation

### Target customers
Service providers (plumbing/HVAC), e-commerce/retail, professional services, local businesses, aspiring entrepreneurs. TAM ~33M SMBs; SAM ~5.6M actively investing.

### Competitor landscape
Indeed, Hootsuite, ChatGPT, LivePlan, ZipRecruiter. Moat = integrated all-in-one + bilingual + low price + community positioning.

### Acquisition model
$5K/mo marketing → ~50K impressions → ~20 new customers → CAC $250 → payback 1.67 months (at $200 ARPU, 75% GM).

### Major 2026 milestones
- Q2: critical product fixes
- Q3: AI Co-Pilot feature release
- Q4: direct multi-channel publishing integrations

### Team
CEO (Andres), 3 engineers, 1 CS manager, 1 content strategist. Current support capacity ~1,200–1,500 users.

### Risks
API dependency, larger competitor feature copying, rising CAC, data privacy, churn.

# Moil — Business Hub

Moil is the AI co-founder for small businesses — one platform that does market research, investor-ready business plans, a 30-day content calendar (Content360), smart hiring across 10+ platforms, AI image/video generation, and 24/7 business coaching. All powered by a 21-question onboarding session. Starting at $15/month. Bilingual EN/ES. SOC 2 compliant.

**Website:** [moilapp.com](https://moilapp.com)
**Entity:** Moil Enterprises Inc. — Buda, Texas
**Founded:** 2023 — Andres Urrego, Founder & CEO

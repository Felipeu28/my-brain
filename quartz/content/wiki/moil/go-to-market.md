## GTM Summary

Moil uses a **community-led + product-led growth (PLG) hybrid** motion. At $15–$75/mo ACV, outbound sales doesn't pencil. The model is:

1. **Meet** owners in person at EDC events, chamber meetings, community gatherings
2. **Demo** the 21-question onboarding live — instant "wow" moment
3. **Let the product convert** — no credit card needed to start, 30-day guarantee removes risk
4. **Expand** from Starter → Professional → Market Pro as the business uses more features

---

## ICP Summary

See [[moil/icp]] for full detail. Short version:

- **Employer side:** Service-based SMBs in Texas, 1–50 employees, $100K–$2M revenue, bilingual workforce, actively hiring. HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, restaurants, salons.
- **Worker side:** Blue-collar and service workers in the same markets, bilingual (EN/ES), underserved by traditional job boards.

The employer pays ($15–$75/mo). The worker is free. Network effects: more candidates → better matches for employers → more employers → more jobs → more candidates.

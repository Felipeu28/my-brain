## Operating Model

Moil operates as a **lean digital platform** with a remote or hybrid team model. Key principles from the operations reference:

- Hire against revenue milestones, not ahead of them
- Prioritize roles that protect product reliability, customer retention, and onboarding quality
- AI handles support at scale (24/7 coaching built into product); human CSM handles edge cases
- One CSM can support ~300 active users → at current ~500 active businesses, this is the next hiring trigger

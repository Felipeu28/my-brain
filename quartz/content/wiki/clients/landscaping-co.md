## Hiring Pain Points

- **Seasonal surge**: needs to hire 5–6 workers in 2–3 weeks every spring
- **Language barrier**: job boards post in English only; crew won't apply
- **No HR staff**: owner is the recruiter, foreman, and accountant
- **Ghost applicants**: Indeed gets clicks, nobody shows up to interviews
- **Turnover**: loses 30–40% of seasonal crew each year

## Why Moil Fits

- Voice-first application removes literacy/language friction
- Spanish-language job posts reach the actual labor pool
- AI pre-screening filters ghosts before owner wastes time
- Text/WhatsApp communication matches crew's phone habits
- $15/mo Starter plan is low-risk for an owner who's never used ATS

## Moil Pricing Tier

**Starter — $15/mo**
- 3 job posts/month (sufficient for seasonal peaks)
- 10+ platform distribution including Spanish job groups
- AI candidate scoring

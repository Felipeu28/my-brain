## Hiring Pain Points

- **Skilled trades shortage**: carpenters, electricians, concrete finishers are hard to source
- **Compliance risk**: I-9 verification, OSHA cert tracking is manual and error-prone
- **Crew ramp-up**: new project starts require 10+ workers in 2 weeks
- **Language mismatch**: safety briefings and job specs are English-only; crew is Spanish-speaking
- **Subcontractor coordination**: tracking who's on-site vs. hired-out is fragmented

## Why Moil Fits

- AI job description generates bilingual (EN/ES) postings automatically
- [[wiki/concepts/smart-hiring]] distributes to 10+ platforms including trade networks
- Voice application catches skilled trades workers who are phone-averse to forms
- Rapid candidate scoring lets foreman interview only the top 3 out of 30 applicants
- $25/mo Professional plan ROI is clear when one bad hire costs $5K+

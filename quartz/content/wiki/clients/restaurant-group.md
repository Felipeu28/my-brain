## Hiring Pain Points

- **BOH turnover**: kitchen staff churn 100–120% annually in Austin market
- **Multi-location complexity**: separate hiring needs per location, no centralized system
- **Speed**: need to fill a line cook role in 48 hours or service breaks down
- **No-shows**: restaurant hiring is plagued by interview ghosting
- **Manager burnout**: GMs are hiring, training, and managing simultaneously

## Why Moil Fits

- Multi-location hiring from one dashboard — assign job posts per location
- 48-hour hire cycle possible: post → AI screens → top 3 candidates in < 24 hours
- WhatsApp interview scheduling dramatically reduces no-shows
- Voice application is natural for kitchen staff who rarely use career sites
- Spanish-first reach for BOH roles; English reach for FOH

## Moil Pricing Tier

**Professional — $25/mo** (per location) or **Enterprise — $75/mo** (all locations)
- Enterprise unlocks unlimited posts + multi-location management

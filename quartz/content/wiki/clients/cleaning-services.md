## Hiring Pain Points

- **Chronic turnover**: 60–80% annual churn is industry norm; always hiring
- **Shift work complexity**: early morning and overnight shifts are hard to staff
- **Background check friction**: many workers lack documents to complete standard checks
- **No visibility into quality**: no way to pre-screen reliability or work history
- **Cost of bad hires**: placing the wrong person in a client's office is a liability

## Why Moil Fits

- Continuous hiring mode = Professional plan pays off immediately
- AI pre-screening catches reliability signals (stability, distance from worksite, hours flexibility)
- Voice application removes the form-filling friction that kills applications from this demographic
- Spanish-first job distribution hits the actual candidate pool
- WhatsApp follow-ups reduce ghosting on interview day

## Hiring Pain Points

- **Volume at scale**: needs 20 workers in 2 weeks for Q4 surge — no system can handle that today
- **Shift fill gaps**: day shift and night shift have different applicant pools; current tools don't differentiate
- **Attrition**: warehouse roles see 80–100% annual turnover; hiring is never-ending
- **Background check delays**: standard checks take 3–5 days; workers accept other jobs by then
- **Manager time sink**: a warehouse HR person spends 60%+ of time on hiring admin

## Why Moil Fits

- Enterprise unlimited posting supports volume hiring without cost explosion
- AI batch scoring — 50 applicants scored and ranked in minutes, not days
- Shift-preference filter: applicants flag day/night preference during voice application
- Speed: candidate pool ready same day, not 72 hours later
- $75/mo Enterprise is a rounding error vs. a $5K temp agency placement fee

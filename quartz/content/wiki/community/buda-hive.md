## What It Is

The Buda HIVE is a co-working space, entrepreneur hub, and B2G relationship anchor in Buda, Texas (Kyle–Buda corridor, south of Austin). It is operated by or affiliated with the Buda Economic Development Corporation (EDC).

For Moil, Buda HIVE is the **most strategic local government relationship** — a real-world sandbox for community hiring programs and potential public workforce partnerships.

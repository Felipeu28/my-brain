## What It Is

Latitud is the leading community and accelerator for Latin American founders building technology companies. Founded in 2021, it runs the **LF (Latitud Fellowship)** cohort program — a selective network of high-growth LatAm and diaspora founders.

Andres is part of **LF6** — the sixth cohort.

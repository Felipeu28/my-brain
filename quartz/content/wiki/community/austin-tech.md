## Overview

Austin is one of the fastest-growing tech hubs in the US. Post-2020, it became a magnet for enterprise tech relocations (Oracle, Tesla, Apple, Dell, Amazon) and a vibrant startup scene. For Moil — a company headquartered in Buda, TX and targeting Austin-area SMBs — the Austin ecosystem is both a **customer market** and a **fundraising network**.

## Investor Landscape

- Austin has a growing seed/Series A ecosystem but is still thin vs. SF/NYC
- Most Austin VCs focus on B2B SaaS, fintech, defense tech
- Hispanic/blue-collar market is underrepresented in Austin VC portfolio → positioning opportunity
- Warm intros via Latitud and Capital Factory are the most reliable path

## Moil's Position in Austin

- Based in Buda (south Austin corridor) — close to key logistics/construction markets
- Active in Buda HIVE (B2G credibility)
- Austin SMBs (landscaping, construction, restaurants) are core Moil ICP
- Austin's booming construction market = high hiring demand = product-market fit signal

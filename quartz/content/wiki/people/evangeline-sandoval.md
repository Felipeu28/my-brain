## Summary

Site Director at TXOR (Texas Office for Refugees) Austin office, which she calls "TechSphere Austin." Key decision-maker for the Moil-TXOR partnership.

## Role & Organization

- **Title:** Site Director, TXOR Austin
- **Organization:** Texas Office for Refugees (TXOR) — federally funded refugee resettlement program
- **Reports to:** Director (unnamed) + federal compliance requirements

## Background

- First-generation immigrant; parents were immigrants. The work is deeply personal to her
- Built the TXOR Austin office from scratch — "everything we've created, we've created from zero"
- Not afraid of testing and failing — explicitly said this is how they found their successful systems
- Strong advocate for client empowerment over dependency: "empower the client, teach the client because we're not always going to be here"

## Relationship to Moil

- First met Andres at a TXOR job fair (before Nov 2024)
- Was impressed by Andres's in-person coaching style with refugees
- Agreed to set up recurring resume-building workshops at TXOR
- Proposed caseworker training so TXOR staff can independently use Moil
- Open to the live hiring podcast concept but needs federal compliance review
- Offered to share Moil info at quarterly consultations with other Austin agencies (potential channel multiplication)

## Key Quotes

- On Andres: "I was really impressed... it meant a lot to me to see the way you carry this profession"
- On testing: "We're here for that... I'm not afraid of testing out systems and them not being successful because ultimately they lead us to the successful systems"
- On empowerment: "You need to empower the client, teach the client because we're not always going to be here"

## Moil Relevance

Evangeline is the operational gatekeeper for the TXOR partnership — the strongest nonprofit channel in Moil's GTM. Her willingness to test and her personal connection to immigrant services makes her a natural champion. She can also introduce Moil to other Austin agencies at quarterly consultations.

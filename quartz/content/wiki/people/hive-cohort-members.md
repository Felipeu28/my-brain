## About the HIVE Program

The HIVE is Buda EDC's entrepreneurial incubator program. Andres serves as the **Moil Business Coach** for HIVE participants — running 1:1 coaching sessions and group workshops. HIVE participants get Moil platform licenses (funded by Buda EDC).

See [[wiki/concepts/hive-program]] for the full 8-week curriculum.

## What she's building (post Apr 30 1:1)

**Pivot:** holistic spa → **women-focused coworking space** ("woman cave" / *"un mancave para mujeres"*). Coworking by day, occasional small events. Spa amenities (sauna, cold plunge, cowboy pool) become add-on perks, not the headline.

**Why this is the right wedge:**
- Buda already has a yoga studio (longstanding), a Meridian spa, and a physical-therapy practice with spinal alignment — adding more wellness would be undifferentiated
- Buda HIVE was originally meant to be a coworking space but couldn't fit — there's an unmet need
- Caro already owns the asset (house is sunk cost) — replaces Airbnb income, doesn't have to exceed it initially
- Cohort 4 already has 4 women + 15 total — built-in waitlist toward the 25-member breakeven

**Tagline she generated organically:** *"un mancave para mujeres."* Connected to her own moment — escapes to the Airbnb house when she can't get more than 40 uninterrupted minutes at home.

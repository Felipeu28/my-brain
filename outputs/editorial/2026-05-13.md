# Daily signal — 2026-05-13

YC spent the week giving Moil's pitch a name and a competitor in the same breath. The "services-as-software" framing is the language to steal; Standout is the company to study before it ships.

## YC is naming your market

### Services-as-software is the phrase to put in the accelerator app

Eric Siu and Alex Vacca both ran the same math: for every $1 SMBs spend on software, they spend $6 on services. That is the exact arbitrage you already pitch — $5K consultant replaced by $25/mo — but in the language YC is now funding around. The accelerator application going in this week and the Buda EDC magazine spread should both swap "AI co-founder" for "services-as-software for SMBs" in at least one paragraph and watch which framing lands better with Jennifer and the cohort.

### Standout is what Moil's candidate side becomes if it doesn't move

YC just funded Standout, an agentic hiring marketplace where AI "turns spam back into signal." Moil already has 50K candidates and 500 businesses on the platform; the live question is whether matching is autonomous yet or still upload-and-wait. If Standout ships first, the candidate-side traffic stops being a moat and becomes a lead-gen funnel for someone else's product. Worth a 30-min Steve sync this week.

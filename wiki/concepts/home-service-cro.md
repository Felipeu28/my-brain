## Summary
A conversion rate optimization (CRO) framework specifically for home service business websites. Source: @noahiglerSEO (Apr 10). Claim: most home service websites convert at less than 1% of visitors; this framework gets clients to an average of 4.6%. The benchmark is significant — 4.6x improvement in conversion rate is transformational for SMB revenue without any increase in traffic spend.

## The 4.6% Conversion Framework (Reconstructed from Signal)
Key principles common to high-converting home service websites:
1. **Above the fold: phone number + "book now" CTA** — home service buyers are ready to call; make it impossible to miss
2. **Trust signals immediately visible** — reviews count, years in business, licensed/insured badges above the scroll line
3. **Clear service area statement** — "Serving Austin, TX and surrounding areas" eliminates bounce from out-of-area visitors
4. **Photo of the actual team/owner** — home services is trust-based; a real face converts better than stock photos
5. **Specific pricing or price range** — "Starting at $X" beats "call for a quote" for initial engagement
6. **Mobile-first design** — 70%+ of home service searches happen on mobile; test on phone, not desktop
7. **Response time promise** — "We call back within 2 hours" converts better than generic contact forms

> **Canonical name as of 2026-04-14.** Moil360 is the customer-facing name for Moil's 30-day AI marketing engine. The internal feature name `Content360` has been deprecated — all references should use Moil360. See [[wiki/concepts/content360]] for the deprecation stub.

## What It Is

Moil360 is Moil's AI-powered 30-day marketing engine. After a customer answers 21 strategic questions, the platform generates a complete monthly content department:

- 30 days of researched topics
- Tested hooks and full captions by content type
- Calls-to-action (CTAs) optimized per post
- Hashtags optimized for each post
- 30 custom AI-generated images (brand-aligned, not stock)
- AI video for highest-impact days (5+ video days included)
- Available in English and Spanish with one-click translation

## Plans & Pricing

- **Starter ($15/mo):** Not included
- **Professional ($25/mo):** Not included
- **Market Pro ($75/mo):** Full Moil360 access + 15 video credits/month

Moil360 is the primary upsell driver from Professional → Market Pro.

## Delivery Modes

Two ways customers consume Moil360:

1. **Self-serve** — customer uses the generated calendar + assets directly (default Market Pro)
2. **Moil360 Content Delivery Service** — [[wiki/people/abiodun-solomon|Ablad]] creates + publishes monthly content using Moil360 output. First-party managed implementation for customers who don't want to execute themselves. [[wiki/orgs/alloy-atx|Alloy ATX]] is the first client on this model.

## Competitive Position

No direct competitor offers this as an integrated package:
- Hootsuite/Buffer do scheduling, not content creation
- ChatGPT/Canva can generate pieces but nothing integrated to business DNA
- Traditional agencies charge $2,000–$5,000/month for comparable output

Moil360 is the feature that makes Moil more than a hiring tool — it's what makes the "AI co-founder" positioning real.

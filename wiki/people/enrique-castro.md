Enrique Castro is the Director of Membership at the Greater Austin Hispanic Chamber of Commerce (GAHCC). He hosts "El Taco Financiero," a bilingual Spanish/English business podcast with Univision and Caracol connections. He was present on the October 2024 GAHCC discovery call with Andres and Monica Munoz Andry.

## Firmographics (must-haves)

| Dimension | Value |
|---|---|
| Industry | Service businesses: HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, landscaping, restaurants, salons, construction, electrical, roofing. Also: e-commerce, professional services (accounting, consulting) |
| Company stage | Operating / established (not pre-revenue). Has revenue, has hired at least once |
| Headcount | 1–50 employees |
| Revenue | $100K–$2M annual |
| Geography | United States, Texas-heavy. Austin metro, Buda, Round Rock, Cedar Park, San Antonio initially |
| Tech stack | Low-tech. Using spreadsheets, manual processes, maybe Indeed for hiring. NOT using Salesforce, HubSpot, or enterprise tools |

## Behavioral signals (the "now" indicator)

- **Actively hiring** — posted a job on Indeed/ZipRecruiter in last 30 days (biggest signal)
- **Searching for marketing help** — Googling "content calendar for small business" or "social media for trades"
- **Attending local events** — shows up at EDC events, chamber meetings, networking nights (like Buda Main Street, Sun show)
- **Recently started or expanding** — new business license filed, second location opening
- **Bilingual workforce** — has Spanish-speaking employees or serves bilingual customers

## Buying committee

| Role | Title patterns | What they care about | How Moil shows up to them |
|---|---|---|---|
| Champion + Economic buyer (same person) | Owner, Founder, GM, "the person who does everything" | Time savings, cost savings, hiring speed | "One platform replaces your consultant, your Indeed account, and your marketing person — for $15/mo" |
| Influencer | Spouse/partner, office manager, bookkeeper | Cost justification, ease of use | 30-day money-back guarantee, no setup fees, "try it free" |
| Blocker | None typical — single decision-maker | "I don't trust AI" or "I'm not tech-savvy" | Voice input in English/Spanish, 5-minute setup, bilingual support |

## Anti-ICP

- **Tech startups** — they already have sophisticated tools, Moil's simplicity isn't a selling point
- **Franchises with corporate marketing** — can't use Content360, hiring is centralized
- **Enterprise (50+ employees)** — need Salesforce/HubSpot-level CRM, Moil isn't built for that
- **Pure e-commerce with no physical presence** — Moil's local hiring and bilingual features don't apply

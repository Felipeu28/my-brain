## Current Website Audit (March 2026)

### Strengths
- Clear brand identity — "Siren" carries mystique and luxury appeal
- Listed on Allē, Fresha, Google, Facebook — foundational local presence
- Community Impact press feature (Nov 2024) — credible backlink + earned media
- Instagram presence establishes visual brand voice
- Philosophical narrative ("sanctuary for intentional living") is compelling positioning
- Historic downtown Buda location — charm differentiator vs. strip-mall medspas
- Latina-owned + locally-owned — authentic community narrative

### Critical Gaps
- **No dedicated service pages** — services not individually indexed by Google or AI
- **Missing SEO fundamentals:** no schema markup (LocalBusiness/MedicalBusiness/Service), thin meta, no H-tag hierarchy
- **No blog/content engine** — zero top-of-funnel capture (e.g. "what is sculptra", "botox vs dysport Buda")
- **No before/after gallery** — highest-converting content type in aesthetics absent
- **No on-site testimonials** — reviews only on 3rd-party platforms
- No transparent pricing, no membership page, no FAQ page (massive AEO gap)
- Limited trust signals: no provider credentials/certifications/story
- No video content, brand story video, or Core Web Vitals optimization verified
- Small IG following (879) limits social proof signals
- Not ranking for "medspa Kyle TX", "medspa San Marcos", "Hays County aesthetics"

### SEO & AEO Status
- **Domain Authority:** New (Oct 2024) — low DA, needs strategic link-building
- **Local Pack Visibility:** Limited — GBP needs complete optimization
- **AI Search Eligibility:** Very low — no structured data, no FAQ, no entity consistency
- **Content Depth:** Minimal — cannot compete with content-rich competitors
- **Backlink Profile:** One earned link (Community Impact) — needs expansion
- **Schema Markup:** None detected — critical 2026 gap

**Overall assessment:** Exceptional brand concept + powerful physical location + compelling philosophy — but the website is not doing justice to the brand. The redesign is not cosmetic; it's a business transformation.

## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Inspiration: Saving Face Austin (savingfaceaustin.com)
Becky admires this regenerative aesthetics brand (3 Austin locations, 25+ team, ~decade in business). Key learnings to adapt:
- Warm home-like aesthetic over clinical white — "approachable luxury"
- Rich editorial photography (not stock)
- Service categories as stories: Solutions / Body & Skin / Injectables / Longevity
- Dedicated "Our Approach" page — philosophy as trust-building content
- Membership with transparent credit-banking model
- Retail shop page (secondary revenue stream)
- Blog/content engine for search traffic + authority
- Conservative injections brand promise: "natural-looking results"
- Packages page — pricing feels curated, not transactional

- Most Hays County residents drive 20–30 min to Austin for quality care
- **Primary capture zone:** Buda, Kyle, Wimberley, Dripping Springs
- **Secondary capture zone:** South Austin, San Marcos, Lockhart
- **Target keywords:** "medspa Buda TX", "botox Kyle TX", "facial Hays County", "injectable near me Buda"
- Positioning sweet spot: more intimate/philosophy-driven than chains, more accessible than Westlake luxury tier

## Moil Project: Website Redesign

### Design Direction
- **Principles:** Atmospheric luxury, editorial over transactional, intentional whitespace, photography-led, motion with restraint, mobile-first luxury
- **Color palette:** Deep Midnight `#1A1A2E`, Warm Gold `#C9A84C`, Muted Rose `#9B6B6B`, Cream `#F5F0EB`, Slate Purple `#4A4A6A`, Pure White `#FFFFFF`
- **Typography:** Cormorant Garamond (display/hero), Playfair Display (headings), DM Sans or Jost (body), Cormorant Italic (pull quotes)
- **Photography:** golden-hour lighting, real skin texture close-ups, interior shots of the Buda space, provider-in-action shots, botanical elements

### Site Architecture (11 pages + 4 local landing pages)
1. Homepage — atmospheric hero, brand statement, service preview grid, differentiators, social proof, before/after preview, IG feed, dual CTA footer
2. **Individual service pages** (10) — each at own slug for SEO:
   - `/services/botox-buda-tx`
   - `/services/lip-filler-buda-tx`
   - `/services/sculptra-buda-tx`
   - `/services/dermal-fillers`
   - `/services/co2-cool-peel-facial`
   - `/services/custom-facials-buda-tx`
   - `/services/dermaplaning`
   - `/services/brow-lamination`
   - `/services/body-composition-scan`
   - `/services/nutritionist-wellness-coaching`
   - *Each page:* hero + what it is + who it's for + what to expect + results timeline + pricing range + 5–7 FAQ + related services + booking CTA + Service/LocalBusiness schema
3. About / Our Story — founder + credentials + the Buda space + press features
4. Gallery / Transformations — before/after slider, consent-collected real results
5. Reviews & Social Proof — aggregated Google/Yelp/direct + video testimonials
6. Membership / Packages — inspired by Saving Face credit-banking model (2–3 tiers)
7. Blog / Education Hub — ≥2 posts/month, E-E-A-T byline
8. FAQ Page — 50+ questions, FAQPage schema for AI Overviews
9. Booking — Fresha-integrated, seamless mobile
10. Contact / Location — styled Google Maps, parking info (free parking!), hours
11. **Local landing pages:** `/buda-tx`, `/kyle-tx`, `/dripping-springs`, `/san-marcos-tx` — each 600+ words, unique FAQ, local references

### SEO & AEO Strategy

**Schema Markup (JSON-LD):**
- LocalBusiness (MedSpa subtype) — NAP, hours, geo, services
- Service schema per service page
- FAQPage schema on FAQ + service pages
- Person schema for provider (E-E-A-T)
- Review/AggregateRating (pulls Google stars into SERPs)
- BreadcrumbList + Article schema

**Core Web Vitals targets:** LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1, INP < 200ms

**Local SEO:**
- Full GBP optimization (all 150+ fields, weekly posts, 2–4 photos/week, 10–15 seeded Q&As, 24hr review response SLA)
- NAP consistency across Google, Yelp, Facebook, Fresha, Allē, Healthgrades, ZocDoc, RealSelf
- Austin Woman Magazine + Community Impact ongoing editorial pursuit
- Buda Chamber of Commerce (see [[wiki/orgs/buda-chamber]])

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) for AI search:**
- Answer-first format on every page
- Conversational query targeting ("How long does botox last in Buda TX?")
- Entity consistency across all properties (AI verifies entity data)
- Heavy FAQ schema — direct pipeline to Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT citations
- Reference: HubSpot 2026 State of Marketing — AI-referred visitors convert 58% higher than traditional organic

### Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router, SSG + ISR)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + custom CSS variables for brand tokens
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **CMS:** Sanity.io (client can edit services/blog/team independently)
- **Booking:** Fresha embed or Jane App
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Search Console
- **Hosting:** Vercel (edge network, zero-config Next.js)
- **Image optimization:** Next.js Image component (WebP + lazy loading)
- **Forms:** React Hook Form + Resend/Postmark
- **Maps:** Google Maps JavaScript API with custom styled map

### Google Stitch 2.0 + Claude Workflow
1. Design brief → Stitch generates visual design system
2. Stitch "vibe design" mode — describe emotional intent (luxury, warmth, sanctuary)
3. Export `Design.md` with all tokens/components/layout rules
4. Connect Stitch MCP → Claude reads design data directly
5. Claude generates Next.js + Tailwind components matching Stitch
6. Iteration loop: refine in Stitch → Claude regenerates
7. Claude integrates Sanity CMS schema
8. Claude implements all JSON-LD schema + meta tags + sitemap
9. Claude runs Lighthouse audits until Core Web Vitals pass
10. Vercel deploy + env + final QA

**Model recommendation:** Sonnet 4.5+ as primary dev model (speed, iteration, cost). Opus for initial architecture, complex SEO implementation, Sanity schema design, final review pass, and long-form service page copy with brand voice.

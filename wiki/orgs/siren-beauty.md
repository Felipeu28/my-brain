## Moil Project: Website Redesign

**Problem:** The current website has strong brand concept but is not commercially performing:
- No individual service pages (Google/AI can't index services)
- No SEO fundamentals (no schema, thin meta, no H-tag hierarchy)
- No content engine, no FAQ (misses AEO opportunity)
- No before/after gallery, no membership page, no testimonial integration
- No GBP optimization strategy

**Deliverables (6-phase, Weeks 1–13):**
1. **Foundation:** Google Stitch 2.0 design system, Sanity CMS schema, GitHub/Vercel setup
2. **Core Architecture:** Next.js 15 App Router, Tailwind brand tokens, homepage, GA4 integration
3. **Service Pages & SEO:** 10 individual service pages, JSON-LD schema, 4 local landing pages (Buda/Kyle/Dripping Springs/San Marcos), XML sitemap
4. **Content Pages:** About, Gallery/before-after, Reviews, Membership/Packages, Contact
5. **Blog & AEO Engine:** Blog with ISR rendering, 6 launch posts, GBP optimization, NAP audit
6. **Launch & QA:** Lighthouse 90+, WCAG 2.1, Vercel production, client Sanity training

**Tech stack:** Next.js 15 + Tailwind CSS + Sanity.io CMS + Framer Motion + Vercel + Google Analytics 4

**KPIs (Month 3–6):** Top 10 for "medspa Buda TX", local 3-pack appearances, AI citation in Perplexity/Google AI Overviews, 15%+ booking conversion rate

## Competitive Context (from strategy doc)

Primary direct competitor: **Fresh Face Aesthetics & Wellness** (Buda, TX) — same geographic market. Siren's differentiation: historic downtown location (not strip mall), Latina-owned identity, wellness integration (nutritionist + body scans).

Inspiration brand: **Saving Face Austin** (3 locations, 25+ team) — studied for editorial website design, membership model, and brand voice. Siren adapts the "feels like home" positioning to "intimate downtown hill country sanctuary."

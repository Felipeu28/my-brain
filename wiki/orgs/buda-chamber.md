## What It Is

The Greater Buda Chamber of Commerce is the local business association for Buda, TX. Distinct from the [[wiki/orgs/buda-edc|Buda EDC]] (government economic development), the Chamber is a member-funded organization focused on networking, events, and business support.

## Relationship with Buda EDC

The Chamber and EDC are separate organizations but collaborate closely. The annual Business Appreciation Breakfast is co-hosted. Jennifer Storm (EDC CEO) and Monica Davidson (Chamber) both have independent relationships with Andres, creating a dual-channel into Buda's business ecosystem.

## What It Is

The Buda EDC is Buda's city-funded economic development corporation. It runs the [[wiki/concepts/buda-hive|Buda HIVE]] entrepreneurship incubator and is pursuing [[wiki/concepts/aedo|AEDO]] accreditation through the IEDC (only 90 orgs worldwide, 12 in Texas). Jennifer Storm was recognized as IEDC Economic Developer of the Year.

## Relationship to Moil

Buda EDC is a **paying B2G customer** — they purchase [[wiki/moil/positioning|Moil]] platform licenses for each [[wiki/concepts/buda-hive|Buda HIVE]] cohort participant. This is recurring per-cohort revenue.

Andres Urrego and [[wiki/people/joshua-edmond|Joshua Edmond]] serve as **Incubator Strategists** under contract — they design and deliver the HIVE curriculum together.

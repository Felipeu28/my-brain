## What It Is

Defense-tech startup building **small directed projectiles** for drone-swarm interception — bringing the per-shot cost of taking down a drone from ~$1M to ~$10K. One of the contractors targeting the **"Gold Dome"** program (the US analog to Israel's Iron Dome that Donald Trump publicly championed).

**Y Combinator cohort:** Summer 2025 (S25).
**Funding raised:** ~$6–8M as of May 2026.
**Headquarters:** Buda, TX.
**Status:** Already in production.

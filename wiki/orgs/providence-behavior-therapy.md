**Founder / clinical lead:** [[wiki/people/heather-skeen|Heather Skeen]] (BCBA)
**Location:** Buda, TX
**Working name change (under review Apr 30):** **Providence Counseling and Therapy** (likely DBA + attorney consult before formal change)

## What they do

Multidisciplinary therapy practice serving primarily children:
- Behavior therapy (Heather, BCBA-led)
- Speech therapy (on-staff therapist)
- Counseling (on-staff counselors)
- ASL tutoring

A **#2 office manager** handles finance.

## Brand origin

Named after **Providence Hospital, Alaska**, where Heather's son spent 3 months in the NICU (born at 35 weeks). Both meanings of "Providence" are intentional — spiritual provision and *"timely preparation for future eventualities."* Definitions are pinned to the website homepage.

## Business model today

- Client-facing therapy hours = primary revenue
- Slow seasons: summer and winter break (kid-driven calendar)
- Already delivered **professional development trainings to 3–4 daycares** in Buda/Kyle (Inspired Minds + others). Standard rate: **$250 per 1-hour presentation per company** (not per attendee)
- One outlier: a **3-hour engagement** discounted to $500 — flagged Apr 30 as a 33% price leak

## Strategic direction (Apr 30 1:1)

**Spin out a training/consulting arm** as a separate LLC ("Providence Consulting") to:
1. Add **passive income** that offsets slow client-facing seasons
2. **Insulate** the clinical practice from training-side liability

**MVP wedge:** annual PD/CEU contracts to daycares — split the 12 required domains across 3–4 sessions for ~$1,000/year per daycare. Local distribution (25-mile radius around Buda), postcard + 3-email sequence + follow-up call.

## Insurance & contracting note

Providence is contracted with insurance companies. Any name change must be cleared with an attorney first — DBA may not be sufficient given the regulated nature of the contracts.

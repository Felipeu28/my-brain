## Differentiation

**Medical credentials.** Most local competitors inject as estheticians without medical training. Nativa positions explicitly as medical care, not aesthetician service. Education content (social + in-person classes) leads with the medical framing.

## Marketing tactics decided May 4

- 30-day, 3–4 posts/week content plan, education-led, with May 21 event CTA
- 10 Facebook groups: 8 high-income Buda neighborhoods (Ruby Ranch, Elliot Ranch, +6 others) + Kyle small-business + women/realtor groups
- 50 flyers distributed only in the 8 target neighborhoods + to Kyle realtors/business cards
- Cross-post personal aesthetic IG (AM) + Nativa IG (PM) — staggered double-reach
- Recurring 1–1.5 hr free education class every 2–4 weeks vs. one-on-one free consults

## Pricing rules

- Replace giveaways with discounted bundles — every "free" thing must trigger a purchase
- Day-of opening: "first 15 free consults, book before you leave" cap; no walk-in personalized consults that day
- "First batch of customers — never offered again" exclusivity bundle on opening-day pricing

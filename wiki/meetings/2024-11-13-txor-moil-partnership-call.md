# TXOR — Moil Partnership Call

**Type:** meeting

## Summary

Follow-up call between Andres and the TXOR (Texas Office for Refugees) Austin team after a successful initial job fair collaboration. The call established a multi-track partnership: recurring resume-building workshops, caseworker training, and a live hiring podcast concept.

## Attendees

- Andres Urrego (andres@moilapp.com)
- Zane Gibson (zgibson@txor.us) — TXOR contact
- Evangeline Sandoval — Site Director, TXOR Austin ("TechSphere Austin")
- Herlinda Rubalcava — PIM (case manager)

## Key Points

1. **TXOR Austin impressed** — Evangeline was at the job fair, saw Andres in action. Got positive feedback from both clients and case managers
2. **Recurring workshops agreed** — Resume-building sessions to be set up as regular (monthly or bimonthly). Federal regulation requires employment program to offer resume-building courses — Moil fills this mandate
3. **Caseworker training** — Andres proposed 1-2 dedicated sessions to train case workers on the Moil platform, so they can independently help refugees
4. **Empowerment philosophy** — Andres's pitch: "What if we switch the mentality to rather than doing for them, we're actually giving them the tools." Resonated strongly with Evangeline
5. **Live hiring podcast** — Andres pitched a live podcast format: 5 companies per week from a specific industry, refugees present on stage, on-the-spot hiring. TXOR interested but needs to review for federal compliance
6. **Federal funding constraint** — TXOR is federally funded; all partnerships must align with federal regulations. Evangeline needs written proposals to review with leadership
7. **Bootstrapping explanation** — Andres explained why he hasn't taken investor money: investors want immediate revenue metrics, but his priority is impact measurement

## Decisions

- Andres to send follow-up email with: (a) written partnership proposal, (b) podcast concept breakdown
- TXOR to pick 2 dates next week (Tue or Wed) for first caseworker training
- Zane to coordinate scheduling

## People Intelligence

- **Evangeline Sandoval** — Site Director of TXOR Austin. First-generation immigrant background. Built the office from scratch ("everything we've created, we've created from zero"). Very receptive to testing and iterating. Key decision-maker
- **Herlinda Rubalcava** — Case manager. Valued the caseworker training angle specifically

## Moil Relevance

This is the partnership that became TXOR's ongoing resume-building program (seen in the April 2025 30+ participant session documented in Run 2). Evangeline's openness to testing and her federal mandate for employment services made TXOR Moil's strongest nonprofit partnership channel.

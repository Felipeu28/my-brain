## Key Decisions — AI Audio Interview Tool

- **Audio-first** (not video): less intimidating, cheaper, faster processing
- **Dual use:** candidates practice + get feedback; businesses screen applicants
- **Analytics:** keywords detected, strengths, red flags, confidence, clarity, overall match quality
- **Candidate view:** Save up to 3 interviews per job title
- **Business view:** Dedicated analytics page; review candidate recordings + summaries
- AI generates questions from job title input (5-15 questions)
- **Profile video script idea:** AI writes a script based on candidate profile → candidate reads it on camera → video shows on their profile for employers
- **Blind hiring:** employers evaluate voice/skill before seeing appearance
- Jacob to research video storage/analytics cost
- Adeleke (Steve) to investigate voice vs. text transcription cost for AI

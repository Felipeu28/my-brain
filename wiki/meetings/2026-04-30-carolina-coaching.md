## Context

First HIVE Cohort 4 1:1 with [[wiki/people/carolina-caro|Caro]]. Caro is "in stand-by professionally" — coming off a Mérida yoga-studio failure that left her hesitant to re-enter entrepreneurship. She and her husband own a house on Austin Street directly behind [[wiki/orgs/meridian-buda|Meridian Buda]], built out for a holistic spa, blocked by City of Buda permitting. Currently runs as Airbnb at ~$2,500/mo target rent (intermittently hit). Husband's hard floor: replace that revenue with whatever business idea wins.

## Key decisions

1. **Drop the holistic spa.** Buda already has a yoga studio (longstanding), a Meridian spa, and a physical-therapy practice with spinal alignment — adding more wellness would be undifferentiated. Caro agreed unprompted.
2. **Pivot to women-focused coworking** — *"un mancave para mujeres"* — coworking by day, occasional small events. The spa amenities (sauna, cold plunge, cowboy pool) become add-on perks, not the headline.
3. **Tiered membership pricing** floated:
   - Basic coworking: $75–$100/mo
   - Mid tier with one event/month included: ~$250/mo (vs. paying separately for venue + catering elsewhere)
   - Annual prepay discount (e.g., $900/yr instead of $1,200) — captures upfront cash, survives slow months
4. **Replace Airbnb income, not exceed it (initially).** 25 women × $100 OR 50 women × $75 ≈ $3,750/mo (covers $2,500 rent + leaves $1,250). Tight but viable.
5. **Outdoor space = expansion lane.** Backyard pergola becomes another usable workspace if enclosed with retractable walls/blinds + AC unit (Andres pointed to **Progress in Austin** as the reference). Sheds in the yard could be converted into 2–3 additional private coworking pods over time. Bathroom permitting was the blocker for the standalone backyard rental — needs a workaround.
6. **Closets become phone booths** (live video tour: Andres flagged the bedroom closets immediately).
7. **Smaller bedroom = bookable conference room.** Reservable for client meetings; foldable wall-mounted tables to flex between conference and open layout.

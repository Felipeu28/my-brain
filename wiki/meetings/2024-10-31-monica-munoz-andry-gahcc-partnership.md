# Monica Munoz Andry — GAHCC Partnership Meeting

## Summary

Andres met with Monica Munoz Andry (Greater Austin Hispanic Chamber of Commerce) and Enrique Castro (Director of Membership). A wide-ranging conversation in Spanish/English about partnering with GAHCC to serve the Hispanic blue-collar community in Austin. Andres expressed interest in GAHCC's Ambassador program and explored collaboration on a bilingual podcast and job outreach to Hispanic-owned businesses.

**Note:** This is a DIFFERENT Monica than Monica Davidson (Buda Chamber). Monica Munoz Andry is at GAHCC (email: monicaa@gahcc.org).

## Key People

- **Monica Munoz Andry** — GAHCC (Greater Austin Hispanic Chamber of Commerce), contact monicaa@gahcc.org
- **Enrique Castro** — Director of Membership at GAHCC; ecastro@gahcc.org; hosts "El Taco Financiero" Spanish-language financial podcast (4+ years); connected with Reykast Media / Felipe
- **Andres Urrego** — Moil founder, bilingual, Latino immigrant background

## What Was Discussed

1. **GAHCC Membership** — Andres agreed to complete G-Membership application; Enrique to send invoice (card or check payment)
2. **Ambassador Program** — Enrique invited Andres to join the ~35-ambassador committee; applications mid-May to mid-July for September cohort
3. **Bilingual Podcast Collaboration** — Andres proposed using GAHCC's platform to send targeted email blasts promoting Moil; suggested Hispanic Chamber branding as credibility signal; Enrique open to podcast collab (his "El Taco Financiero" podcast)
4. **EGBI Connection** — Both parties discussed EGBI (related nonprofit that Andres also works with); Enrique noted EGBI spun out of Hispanic Chamber ~20-25 years ago; Larissa leads EGBI
5. **Spanish-language products** — Andres noted Moil will be 100% bilingual (CRM + website in Spanish); emphasized the Latino SMB contractor segment (construction, cleaning, food) as core market
6. **Partnership vision** — Andres proposed: GAHCC provides the community network and media; Moil provides the platform and candidate/job matching. Andres handles financing, marketing, candidate acquisition.

## Decisions

- Andres joining GAHCC membership (paid)
- Follow-up call scheduled to explore deeper partnership
- Potential Univision/Caracol promotion (free first 3-4 podcast appearances to avoid paid content restrictions)

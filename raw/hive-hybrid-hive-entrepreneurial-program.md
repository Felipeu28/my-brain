Hybrid HIVE Entrepreneurial Program
4.1 Hatch Stage (Idea Stage)
Target Audience: Entrepreneurs with ideas but no formal business.Format: 6-week series (weekly 2-hour workshops, LMS + in-person at Buda HIVE).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Ideation and problem-solution fit.
Week 2: Market research (surveys, competitors).
Week 3: Business concept development.
Week 4: Business planning
Week 5: Legal and regulatory compliance basics.
Week 6: Finalize plan, pitch practice.
Tools: SBA templates, need to add more
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Registered business with completed business plan.
4.2 Initiate Stage (Startup Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses launching or recently launched.Format: 6-week series (weekly 2-hour workshops).Support Provided:
Workshops:
Week 1: Product development basics.
Week 2: MVP creation and testing.
Week 3: Customer acquisition strategies.
Week 4: Financial management (QuickBooks).
Week 5: Branding and marketing.
Week 6: Revenue planning and review.
Resources: Texas SBDC coaching, QuickBooks licenses.
Mentorship: 1:1 CoachingMilestone: Operational business with initial revenue.
4.3 Validate Stage (Established Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses with consistent operations.Format: 2-day intensive (16 hours, tailored to operational needs “‘ focus on efficiency and growth”).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Day 1: Lean process optimization, CRM setup (HubSpot).
Day 2: Advanced marketing (SEO, analytics), financial forecasting.
Resources: CRM trial, marketing templates.
Mentorship: 2-hour group session with scaling experts.Milestone: Optimized operations with growth metrics (e.g., 10% efficiency or revenue growth).
4.4 Expand Stage (Scale Stage)
Target Audience: Businesses ready to scale or enter new markets.Format: 2-day intensive (16 hours, tailored to strategic scaling).Support Provided:
Workshop Agenda:
Day 1: Growth strategies (new markets, partnerships), capital access (pitch coaching).
Day 2: Workforce planning, long-term strategic roadmap.
Resources: Capital Factory investor intros, Texas Woman’s University training.
Mentorship: 2-hour session with investors and strategists.Milestone: Investor-ready pitch and scaling roadmap.

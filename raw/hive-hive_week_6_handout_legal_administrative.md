Step 1: Choose Your Business Structure
WORKSHOP ACTIVITY
Compare structures and choose the best for YOUR business:
Sole Proprietorship:
Simplest, you = business, personal liability, easy taxes
LLC (Recommended):
Personal protection, flexible taxes, professional credibility
S Corporation:
Tax savings for higher earners, more paperwork, payroll required
C Corporation:
For high-growth/investor businesses, double taxation, complex
Partnership:
Multiple owners, shared liability, partnership agreement needed
Nonprofit:
Mission-driven, tax-exempt status, strict regulations

Step 2: Registration Checklist
Complete these steps in order:
☐ Choose and register business name (check Texas SOS availability)
☐ File formation documents with Texas Secretary of State
☐ Get Federal EIN (Employer Identification Number) from IRS
☐ Register for Texas sales tax permit (if selling physical goods)
☐ Register with Texas Comptroller for franchise tax
☐ Get City of Buda business license/permit
☐ Get Hays County permits (if applicable)
☐ Register for Texas unemployment insurance (if hiring)
☐ Get industry-specific licenses (food, health, professional, etc.)
Buda Resources:
City of Buda Economic Development: developbuda.com
Texas Secretary of State: sos.texas.gov
IRS EIN: irs.gov/ein
Texas Comptroller: comptroller.texas.gov

Why Validate Your Idea?
Most startups fail because they build something nobody wants. Validation helps you test your assumptions BEFORE you waste time and money!
Key Stats:
42% of startups fail due to no market need
Validated startups are 60% more likely to succeed
Customer interviews cost $0 but save thousands

Step 3: Customer Discovery Interviews
This is THE MOST IMPORTANT validation step. Talk to real potential customers!
Interview Goal:
5-10 interviews minimum
15-30 minutes each
In-person is best (video call second best)
Listen 80%, talk 20%
CRITICAL RULE:
DO NOT pitch your solution! Ask about their PROBLEM first.
Interview Script Template:
OPENING (2 minutes):
"Hi [Name]! Thanks for talking with me. I am exploring a business idea and want to understand [problem area]. Can I ask you a few questions?"
PROBLEM DISCOVERY (10 minutes):
1. Tell me about the last time you [relevant situation]?
2. What was frustrating about that experience?
3. How are you currently solving this problem?
4. What do you wish existed to make this easier?
SOLUTION VALIDATION (5-10 minutes):
"What if there was [brief solution description]?"
1. Would that solve your problem?
2. What concerns would you have?
3. Would you pay for this? How much?
CLOSING (3 minutes):
"Who else should I talk to about this?"
"Can I follow up with you as I develop this?"

Step 4: Build a Simple Prototype (MVP)
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) - the simplest version of your idea to test with real customers.
MVP Examples:
Food business - Cook 5 meals, give to friends for feedback
Service business - Offer 3 free sessions in exchange for testimonials
Product business - Create sketches or 3D mockups to show
Digital business - Build a simple landing page describing it

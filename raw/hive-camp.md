AI for Small Business
Buda 2025 Entrepreneur Camp | June 18, 2025
Panelists: Andres Urrego (MoilApp), Joshua Edmond (JUCO AI)
Objective: Empower SMBs with AI tools to automate, scale, and grow confidently. Hit key messages: “No question unanswered in 2025,” “Make AI become you,” “Sky’s the limit.” Promote MoilApp’s free tools.
Time: ~50–55 min (3 + 5 + 16 + 5 + 3 + 15 + 2 + 5–10)
Slides: 1–2 per section (image1.png–image19.png).
1. Opening Remarks (3 min, Andres, Slide 1)
Goal: Set tone, spark urgency. Slide: Title, panelist intros.
“Welcome! I’m Andres (MoilApp, offline workers/SMBs). Josh is with JUCO AI (student success). We’re here to equip you with AI.”
“SMBs juggle tight budgets, small teams. MoilApp’s AI cut hiring time—game-changer!”
Impact: “2025: No question unanswered. Grok, ChatGPT give instant answers. Don’t miss AI like we did Google. Sky’s the limit!”
“We’ll cover AI basics, tools, a live landing page demo, MoilApp free tools.”
Engage: “Raise hand if tasks overwhelm you.” Transition: “Josh, define AI; I’ll add prompting.”
2. What is AI? (5 min, Josh leads, Andres on Prompting, Slides 2–3)
Goal: Demystify AI, teach prompting. Slide 2: AI examples. Slide 3: Prompt structure.
Josh (2 min): “AI mimics human thinking—learns, predicts, creates. Think GPS, Netflix. Predictive AI forecasts; Generative AI (ChatGPT, Grok) creates.”
Andres (3 min):
“Prompts drive AI results: Role (‘web designer’), Task (‘landing page’), Details (‘coffee shop’), Tone (‘friendly’), Constraints (‘200 words’).”
Impact: “Ex: ‘Write a bakery social post, families, warm, no jargon.’ Restaurant saved hours with Grok!”
Activity: “Pair up, draft a prompt (30 sec).”
Tools: ChatGPT, Grok (free on x.com), Claude, Gemini. Engage: “Who’s used AI?” Transition: “Josh, operations next.”
3. Practical AI Applications (16 min, Slides 4–7)
Goal: Show AI for SMB pain points.
3a. Marketing (8 min, Josh leads, Slides 4–5)
Slides: Strategy, tools (chatbots, emails, content).
Listen: Strategy first (avoid noise, e.g., gym ads for members). Chatbots save 30% costs. Mailchimp AI boosts email clicks 20%. OpusClip, HeyGen speed content 80%.
Support: “MoilApp’s AI ads doubled engagement by targeting right workers.”
Tools: Mailchimp AI, OpusClip, HeyGen, Grok. Engage: Josh asks, “Marketing time a struggle?” Transition: “Andres, take operations.”
3b. Operations (8 min, Andres, Slides 6–7)
Slides: Inventory, HR, financials.
“Predictive AI cuts stockouts 15%—restaurant saved $5,000. QuickBooks AI tracks expenses real-time. MoilApp AI screens resumes, cuts hiring 30%. AI routes save 5-10% fuel.”
Impact: “Lean teams do more with less.”
Tools: QuickBooks AI, MoilApp HR AI (free 3 weeks). Engage: “Who’s bogged down by hiring?” Transition: “Let’s start using AI.”
4. Getting Started: Tools (5 min, Andres, Slide 8)
Goal: Easy roadmap. Slide: Tool table.
“Pick one task (emails, scheduling). Use Microsoft 365 Copilot. Try free ChatGPT, Grok, Canva. Pilot $500–$2,000, measure 30 days.”
Impact: “Make AI you—question outputs, tweak to your brand, dig deeper.”
Tools: ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini (prompting), Flowrite/Mailr (emails), Perplexity (research), MoilApp Plan Creator (free 3 weeks), Canva/Looka (design), OpusClip/HeyGen (social), MoilApp Hiring (free).
Engage: “What task to automate?” Transition: “Now, AI challenges.”
5. Challenges (3 min, Andres, Slide 9)
Goal: Build trust. Slide: Privacy, bias, training.
“68% worry about data—use Grok, Claude (GDPR-safe). Check AI bias. Pick QuickBooks, Shopify for integration. Train one person via Coursera, Udemy.”
Tools: Grok, Claude, Coursera, Udemy. Engage: “Save concerns for Q&amp;A.” Transition: “Time for the demo!”
6. Live Demo: Landing Page (15 min, Andres, Slide 10)
Goal: Show AI’s power. Slide: Prompt, live interface.
“We’ll build a coffee shop page in 15 min with Grok/ChatGPT.”
Ask (1 min): “Offer? Customer? Unique? Price?” (Default: coffee shop, young pros, cozy, $3–$5.)
Prompt (1 min): “Web designer, coffee shop page, young pros, cozy, $3–$5, headline, subheadline, call-to-action, ‘About Us,’ ‘Menu,’ friendly, &lt;300 words, HTML.”
Demo (10 min): Input prompt, show HTML, paste in CodePen, display. “Headline grabs, call-to-action sells!”
Impact: “No coding needed—tweak for your business.”
Tools: Grok, ChatGPT, CodePen. Engage: “Volunteer a business type?” Transition: “MoilApp news next.”
7. Moil Announcement (2 min, Andres, Slide 11)
Goal: Promote tools. Slide: MoilApp logo, free tools.
Impact: “MoilApp’s AI Business Plan Creator: Free 3 weeks, pro plan in &lt;1 hour. AI Hiring Platform: Free now, cuts hiring 30%.”
“Visit moilapp.com—solve time, resources.”
Tools: MoilApp Plan Creator, Hiring Platform. Engage: “Need a plan or hires?” Transition: “Q&amp;A time.”
8. Q&amp;A + Wrap-Up (5–10 min, Andres &amp; Josh, Slide 12)
Goal: Inspire action. Slide: Contacts, resources.
Q&amp;A (3–7 min): “Ask anything—2025, no question unanswered!” (Ex: “Best tool?” Grok/ChatGPT. “Errors?” Test small.)
Wrap-Up (2–3 min): “Test Grok, MoilApp tools 90 days. Learn via Coursera. Email hive@budaedc.com.”
Impact: “Sky’s the limit—don’t miss AI. Start today!”
Tools: Grok, ChatGPT, MoilApp, Coursera. Engage: “Commit to one tool—share at networking!”
Tips: Start strong, transition smoothly (“Josh, your turn”). Andres leads Opening, Operations, Getting Started, Challenges, Demo, Moil, co-leads Q&amp;A. Josh leads What is AI (part), Marketing, co-leads Q&amp;A. Use timer for 55 min. Handout: Tools (Grok, ChatGPT, MoilApp, etc.), prompt structure, hive@budaedc.com.

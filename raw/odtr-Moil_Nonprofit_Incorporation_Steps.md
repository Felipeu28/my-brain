## Steps for Incorporating Moil Empowerment Foundation

## Step-by-Step Incorporation Process
1. Decide on the Name:- Verify the availability of your nonprofit name (e.g., Moil Empowerment Foundation) through the Texas Secretary of State.
2. Draft and File the Certificate of Formation:- Include the following:  - Nonprofit name.  - Purpose (aligned with 501(c)(3) requirements).  - Registered agent's information (someone in Texas to receive official correspondence).  - Initial board of directors (at least three members).
3. Obtain an Employer Identification Number (EIN):- A separate EIN is required for the nonprofit, distinct from Moil Enterprise Inc.- Apply online via the IRS website.
4. Create Bylaws:- Define how the nonprofit will operate, including:  - Governance structure.  - Board roles and responsibilities.  - Financial policies and meeting schedules.
5. Apply for 501(c)(3) Status:- File IRS Form 1023 or Form 1023-EZ for tax-exempt status.- Include a narrative description of your programs and financial projections.
6. Set Up Financial Systems:- Open a separate bank account for the nonprofit.- Implement accounting practices to maintain clear financial separation from Moil Enterprise Inc.
7. Register with the Texas Comptroller:- Apply for exemption from state franchise and sales taxes.
8. Develop Initial Programs:- Start planning your first webinars and workshops to demonstrate impact.

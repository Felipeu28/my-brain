Why Finance Matters
You can have the best product in the world, but if your finances are a mess, your business will fail. Understanding basic finance is NOT optional for entrepreneurs!

Step 3: Pricing Strategy
WORKSHOP ACTIVITY
Calculate your pricing using the Cost-Plus method:
1. Cost per Unit (materials + labor + overhead):
Direct materials cost: $__________
Direct labor cost: $__________
Overhead per unit: $__________
TOTAL COST PER UNIT: $__________
2. Desired Profit Margin (%):
Industry standard (research): ________%
Your target margin: ________%
3. Calculate Price:
Formula: Price = Cost / (1 - Desired Margin %)
Example: $10 cost with 40% margin = $10 / 0.6 = $16.67 price
YOUR PRICE: $__________
4. Competitive Check:
Competitor 1 price: $__________
Competitor 2 price: $__________
Competitor 3 price: $__________
Is your price competitive? If not, adjust margin or costs.
Remember:
Don't compete on price alone! Compete on value, quality, and service.
